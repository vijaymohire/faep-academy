# FAEP Mission Patterns

## Overview

The **FAEP Mission Patterns** library provides reusable engineering patterns for designing, coordinating and executing mission-oriented intelligent systems within the Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform (FAEP).

Mission patterns define standardized approaches for organizing objectives, coordinating resources, orchestrating intelligent services and achieving measurable outcomes across enterprise, scientific and cyber-physical environments.

Within the QAI Computing Foundations, Mission Patterns integrate knowledge, decision-making, intelligent agents, digital twins, orchestration and hybrid classical-quantum computing into reusable mission execution models.

I think Mission Patterns are actually one of the most distinctive concepts in your entire FAEP ecosystem.

Unlike workflow patterns or business process patterns, Mission Patterns are goal-oriented engineering patterns that coordinate multiple capabilities—agents, knowledge, decision-making, digital twins, runtimes and orchestration—to achieve a measurable objective.

This aligns perfectly with your earlier work on Mission Runtime, Mission Control Plane, Mission Registry, Mission Orchestration and Mission Digital Twins.
