# Labs

> **FAEP Education | Hands-on Laboratories for Quantum Artificial Intelligence**

---

# Overview

The **Labs** repository provides practical, hands-on learning experiences that enable learners to design, build, experiment with, validate, and deploy technologies across the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

Unlike traditional tutorials that focus on isolated concepts, FAEP Labs integrate multiple technologies including Artificial Intelligence, Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI), Digital Twins, Enterprise Intelligence, Hybrid Runtime, Knowledge Graphs, Service Mesh, and Enterprise Architecture into realistic engineering scenarios.

Labs provide an environment for experimentation, innovation, skill development, and enterprise adoption.

---

# Vision

Develop a comprehensive laboratory ecosystem that enables learners, researchers, engineers, startups, universities, and enterprises to gain practical experience in designing and deploying future Quantum Artificial Intelligence solutions.
