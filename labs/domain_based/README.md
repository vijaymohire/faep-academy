# Overview

The **Domain-Based Labs** repository provides practical, hands-on learning experiences for applying the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) to real-world industries, scientific disciplines, government sectors, and enterprise domains.

Unlike technology-focused laboratories that concentrate on individual tools or platforms, Domain-Based Labs emphasize solving business and mission problems by integrating Artificial Intelligence (AI), Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI), Enterprise Intelligence, Digital Twins, Knowledge Graphs, Hybrid Runtime, Enterprise Architecture, and Systems Engineering into complete domain-specific solutions.

These laboratories demonstrate how a common engineering foundation can be adapted to diverse industries through reusable architectures, domain knowledge, and enterprise best practices.

# Why Domain-Based Laboratories?

Every industry presents unique operational challenges, regulatory requirements, business processes, and mission objectives.

Rather than building isolated solutions for every domain, FAEP promotes reusable enterprise architectures that can be customized using domain-specific knowledge, policies, data models, and operational workflows.

This approach reduces development effort while improving interoperability, scalability, and long-term maintainability.
