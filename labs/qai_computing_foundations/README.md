# QAI Computing Foundations Lab

> **Building the Scientific, Engineering, and Computing Foundations for the Next Generation of Quantum Artificial Intelligence**

---

# Overview

The **QAI Computing Foundations Lab** provides a comprehensive engineering environment for exploring the scientific, mathematical, computational, and architectural principles that underpin Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI).

Unlike traditional quantum computing laboratories that primarily focus on quantum circuits and algorithms, this laboratory adopts a systems engineering perspective that integrates classical computing, Artificial Intelligence, Quantum Computing, enterprise architectures, hybrid computing, and future intelligent ecosystems into a unified learning experience.

The laboratory emphasizes practical engineering, experimentation, architectural thinking, and continuous innovation.

---

# Vision

To establish a modern educational and engineering laboratory that bridges scientific research, enterprise engineering, Quantum Computing, Artificial Intelligence, and future Quantum Artificial Intelligence technologies.

The laboratory prepares learners for emerging computing paradigms ranging from today's AI-assisted systems to future Fault-Tolerant Quantum Computing (FTQC), Quantum Artificial General Intelligence (QAGI), and Quantum Artificial Super Intelligence (QASI).
