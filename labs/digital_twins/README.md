# Overview

The **Digital Twins** laboratory provides practical, hands-on learning experiences for designing, developing, validating, and operating Digital Twins across enterprise, industrial, scientific, and mission-critical environments.

Within the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform), Digital Twins extend far beyond visualization or simulation. They become intelligent digital representations that integrate Artificial Intelligence (AI), Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI), Enterprise Intelligence, Knowledge Graphs, Hybrid Runtime, Runtime Services, cloud platforms, and real-time operational data.

These laboratories enable learners to engineer Digital Twin ecosystems that continuously observe, learn, predict, optimize, and support enterprise decision-making.

This is one of the strongest topics in the FAEP ecosystem because Digital Twins are not simply simulations—they become the digital representation of enterprise knowledge and operational intelligence.
