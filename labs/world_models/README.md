# Overview

The **QAI World Models Lab** introduces the engineering principles, architectures, computational models, and knowledge representations required to build intelligent digital models of real-world systems.

Rather than viewing Artificial Intelligence as isolated algorithms, this laboratory treats intelligent systems as comprehensive representations of environments, organizations, scientific domains, engineering systems, and Federated Autonomous Ecosystems.

The laboratory integrates Artificial Intelligence, Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI), Systems Engineering, Digital Twins, Knowledge Graphs, Scientific Computing, Hybrid Computing, Robotics, Enterprise Architecture, and Engineering Validation into a unified framework for understanding and reasoning about complex environments.
