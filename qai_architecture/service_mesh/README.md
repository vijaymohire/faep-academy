# Overview

The **Service Mesh** repository defines the intelligent communication fabric responsible for securely connecting, coordinating, governing, and optimizing enterprise services across the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform).

Unlike traditional service meshes that primarily focus on microservice communication, the FAEP Service Mesh interconnects enterprise services, Digital Twins, AI models, runtime services, hybrid computing resources, control planes, and distributed infrastructures through an adaptive and policy-driven communication fabric.

//

One concept that naturally emerges from your architecture is the idea of an Enterprise Fabric Architecture. Rather than treating the Service Mesh, Data Fabric, Knowledge Graph, Digital Twin synchronization, Runtime Services, and QAI Hub as separate technologies, they can be viewed as specialized fabrics within a larger ecosystem. Each fabric has a focused responsibility—communication, knowledge, execution, synchronization, or governance—but they cooperate through common registries, control planes, and enterprise policies. This layered "fabric of fabrics" approach would provide a unifying architectural language for FAEP and could become one of its distinguishing characteristics as the platform grows.
