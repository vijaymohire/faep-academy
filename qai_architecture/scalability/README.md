# Overview

The **Scalability** repository provides a comprehensive architectural framework for designing, deploying, governing, and continuously expanding Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI) ecosystems.

Unlike traditional scalability that focuses primarily on infrastructure growth, FAEP treats scalability as the coordinated expansion of enterprise capabilities including intelligence, compute, Digital Twins, Knowledge Graphs, hybrid runtimes, autonomous agents, enterprise services, and mission operations.

Scalability therefore becomes an enterprise capability that enables ecosystems to evolve from local deployments to globally distributed and future space-based infrastructures while maintaining integrity, governance, resilience, and operational continuity.
