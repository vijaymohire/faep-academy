# Overview

The **Observability** repository provides a comprehensive architectural framework for understanding, monitoring, analyzing, and optimizing enterprise-scale Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI) platforms.

Modern enterprise systems generate enormous volumes of operational information across applications, cloud platforms, AI models, Quantum Artificial Intelligence services, Digital Twins, Knowledge Graphs, enterprise integrations, and distributed infrastructure.

Rather than simply collecting telemetry, FAEP extends observability into **Enterprise Operational Intelligence**, enabling systems to understand operational behavior, predict emerging issues, recommend corrective actions, and support autonomous enterprise operations.

Traditionally, observability is explained as the "three pillars":

Logs
Metrics
Traces

Today it has expanded to include:

Events
Telemetry
Profiling
Distributed tracing

But I think FAEP should go much further.

Your platform already contains:

These naturally lead to Intelligent Observability.

Instead of simply collecting telemetry, the platform should understand:

I think this concept fits naturally with the FAEP philosophy of evolving from monitoring to enterprise operational intelligence and ultimately toward governed autonomous operations.
