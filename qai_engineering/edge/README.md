# Overview

The **Edge Engineering** repository focuses on designing, developing, deploying, and operating intelligent computing systems at or near the source of data generation within the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

Edge Engineering combines edge computing, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI), Digital Twins, Knowledge Graphs, IoT, robotics, cloud platforms, and cyber-physical systems to enable intelligent real-time decision-making while reducing latency, bandwidth consumption, and dependence on centralized cloud resources.

The objective is to build scalable, secure, resilient, and distributed intelligent systems capable of supporting enterprise modernization and future Quantum AI applications.

Excellent. Edge Engineering is becoming increasingly important because intelligent systems are no longer confined to centralized cloud environments. Within the FAEP ecosystem, Edge Engineering serves as the bridge between physical assets, Digital Twins, cloud platforms, AI, and future Quantum AI infrastructures.

I would define it as the engineering discipline responsible for delivering low-latency, distributed intelligence close to where data is generated.
