# Deployment Engineering

> **FAEP QAI Engineering | Deployment & Operations | Enterprise AI & Quantum AI Lifecycle**

---

# Overview

The **Deployment Engineering** repository focuses on designing, validating, deploying, operating, monitoring, and continuously improving Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI) systems within the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

Deployment Engineering transforms research prototypes into secure, scalable, resilient, and production-ready enterprise solutions. It integrates DevSecOps, MLOps, QAIOps, cloud-native platforms, Digital Twins, Knowledge Graphs, automation, governance, and continuous delivery into a unified deployment framework.

The objective is to establish repeatable engineering practices that enable reliable delivery of intelligent enterprise systems.

---

# Vision

Develop reusable deployment engineering practices that enable organizations to rapidly, securely, and reliably deploy AI and Quantum AI solutions across enterprise, cloud, edge, and hybrid computing environments.
