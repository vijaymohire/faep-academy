# Overview

The **Enterprise Engineering** repository provides a comprehensive engineering framework for designing, integrating, governing, deploying, and continuously improving Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI) capabilities across enterprise environments.

Enterprise Engineering extends traditional enterprise architecture by integrating Digital Twins, Knowledge Graphs, AI, Quantum AI, cloud platforms, cyber-physical systems, governance, security, compliance, and modernization into a unified enterprise operating model.

The objective is to transform organizations into intelligent, adaptive, resilient, and continuously evolving enterprises capable of adopting next-generation technologies.

This is probably the most strategic engineering domain in the entire QAI Engineering repository.

The other engineering domains build technology. Enterprise Engineering answers:

"How do we integrate, govern, scale, secure, modernize, and continuously improve these technologies across an enterprise?"

It is also the bridge between QAI Engineering and the frameworks we've recently developed (FAEP, EPCRM, HEEA, FAEF, QAITF).
