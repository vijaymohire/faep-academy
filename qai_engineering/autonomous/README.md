# Overview

The **Autonomous Engineering** repository focuses on the design, development, validation, deployment, and governance of autonomous systems within the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

Autonomous systems combine Artificial Intelligence (AI), Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI), Digital Twins, Knowledge Graphs, robotics, sensors, cloud computing, and edge computing to enable intelligent perception, reasoning, planning, and action.

The objective is to develop trustworthy autonomous systems that improve productivity, safety, resilience, and decision-making while supporting appropriate human oversight and governance.

---

# Vision

Develop reusable engineering principles, architectures, and best practices for intelligent autonomous systems that can operate across enterprise, industrial, scientific, and future Quantum AI environments.
