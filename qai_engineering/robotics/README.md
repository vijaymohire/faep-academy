# Overview

The **Robotics Engineering** repository provides a comprehensive engineering framework for designing, developing, validating, deploying, and operating intelligent robotic systems within the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

Modern robotics extends beyond mechanical systems by integrating Artificial Intelligence (AI), Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI), Digital Twins, Knowledge Graphs, Cyber-Physical Systems, cloud computing, edge computing, sensors, and autonomous decision-making into intelligent robotic platforms.

The objective is to develop safe, adaptable, collaborative, and autonomous robotic systems capable of supporting enterprise modernization, industrial transformation, scientific research, healthcare, and future space exploration.

I think Robotics Engineering should not be viewed simply as robot development. Instead, within the FAEP ecosystem, it becomes the engineering discipline that combines AI, Quantum AI, Digital Twins, Cyber-Physical Systems, Edge Computing, Cloud Computing, and intelligent autonomy to build next-generation robotic systems.
