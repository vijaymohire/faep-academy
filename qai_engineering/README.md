# Overview

The **QAI Engineering** repository provides a comprehensive engineering framework for designing, developing, validating, deploying, operating, and governing Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI) systems within the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

QAI Engineering extends traditional software and systems engineering by integrating classical computing, quantum computing, Digital Twins, Knowledge Graphs, cloud platforms, robotics, cyber-physical systems, and intelligent automation into a unified engineering discipline.

The objective is to transform scientific innovation into scalable, secure, maintainable, and enterprise-ready solutions.

---

# Vision

Develop a reusable engineering framework that enables organizations to build intelligent, trustworthy, scalable, and interoperable AI and Quantum AI systems supporting enterprise modernization and industrial transformation.
