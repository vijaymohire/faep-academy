# Overview

The **Cyber-Physical Engineering** repository focuses on designing, developing, integrating, validating, deploying, and operating Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS) within the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

Cyber-Physical Systems combine physical assets, sensors, embedded systems, communication networks, cloud platforms, Digital Twins, Artificial Intelligence (AI), and Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI) into intelligent connected environments capable of monitoring, reasoning, automation, and autonomous operation.

This repository provides engineering guidance for developing safe, resilient, secure, and intelligent cyber-physical infrastructures across industrial, enterprise, scientific, healthcare, transportation, and future space systems.

I think Cyber-Physical Engineering is one of the most important engineering disciplines because it is where the digital world meets the physical world.

Within the FAEP ecosystem, I would broaden it beyond Industrial IoT to include intelligent infrastructure, Digital Twins, robotics, edge computing, AI, and future Quantum AI-enabled physical systems.
