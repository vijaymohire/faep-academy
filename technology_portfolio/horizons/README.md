# Technology Horizons

> **FAEP Technology Portfolio | Strategic Technology Planning | Portfolio Governance | Innovation Horizons & Long-Term Enterprise Growth**

---

# Overview

The **Technology Horizons** repository provides a strategic framework for planning, governing, prioritizing, and continuously evolving enterprise technologies across multiple investment horizons within the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

Technology Horizons enable organizations to balance short-term revenue generation with long-term research, engineering, innovation, commercialization, and enterprise modernization.

Rather than treating innovation as isolated projects, Technology Horizons establish a continuously evolving portfolio management process that transforms research into sustainable enterprise growth.

The biggest evolution is that Technology Horizons is no longer just a timeline—it becomes the Portfolio Planning & Governance Framework that executives can use to continuously steer the organization.
---
