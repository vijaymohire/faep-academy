# Overview

The **Technology Portfolio** repository provides a comprehensive framework for managing technology assets, research initiatives, engineering capabilities, products, services, investments, commercialization strategies, and innovation roadmaps within the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

Rather than acting as a static technology inventory, the Technology Portfolio serves as a strategic decision-support framework that enables organizations to rapidly assemble capabilities, respond to new opportunities, prioritize investments, and transform research into sustainable business value.

It integrates Knowledge Graphs, Digital Twins, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI), enterprise architecture, modernization, and innovation management into a reusable portfolio management framework.

I think after today's discussion the Technology Portfolio has evolved beyond a technology catalog. It has become the strategic decision-making layer of the FAEP ecosystem.

Rather than simply documenting technologies, it now answers:

"Given an opportunity, how do we assemble the right capabilities to create value?"
