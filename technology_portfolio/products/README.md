# Overview

The **Products** repository provides a comprehensive framework for managing enterprise products, platforms, services, frameworks, digital assets, and commercial offerings within the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

Rather than managing isolated products, the Product Portfolio manages reusable enterprise capabilities that can be assembled into customer solutions, modernization programs, educational offerings, and strategic partnerships.

The objective is to maximize reuse, accelerate commercialization, improve product governance, and continuously evolve enterprise offerings.

I think this is now one of the flagship repositories of the FAEP Academy because it represents the enterprise offerings that emerge from everything you've built.

The biggest conceptual change is this:

The Product Portfolio is not merely a list of products—it is the enterprise capability catalog.

It answers:

What can we sell?
What can we license?
What can we teach?
What can we customize?
What can we modernize?
What can partners leverage?
What is currently in research?
