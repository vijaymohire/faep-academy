# Innovation Case Studies

This collection provides curated case studies demonstrating the practical application of Innovation Engineering across technology development, applied research, product engineering, digital transformation, entrepreneurship, intellectual property, and enterprise innovation.

These case studies complement the FAEP Academy courses, tutorials, laboratories, notebooks, and engineering projects.

---

# Objectives

This collection helps learners:

- Understand structured innovation methodologies
- Study engineering-driven innovation processes
- Learn from successful technology and product development
- Analyze innovation challenges and engineering decisions
- Evaluate commercialization strategies
- Explore emerging innovation practices
