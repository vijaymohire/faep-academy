# Artificial Intelligence Case Studies

This collection provides curated case studies demonstrating the practical application of Artificial Intelligence across research, engineering, enterprise transformation, industrial automation, healthcare, finance, manufacturing, cybersecurity, and scientific computing.

These case studies complement the FAEP Academy courses, tutorials, laboratories, notebooks, and engineering projects.

---

# Objectives

This collection helps learners:

- Understand real-world AI applications
- Study engineering design decisions
- Learn from successful AI deployments
- Analyze implementation challenges
- Evaluate engineering trade-offs
- Explore emerging AI technologies
