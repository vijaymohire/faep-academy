# Tacit Knowledge Case Studies

This collection provides curated case studies demonstrating the practical application of Tacit Knowledge across engineering organizations, research laboratories, enterprise transformation, product development, innovation management, organizational learning, and knowledge-centric systems.

These case studies complement the FAEP Academy courses, tutorials, laboratories, notebooks, and engineering projects.

---

# Objectives

This collection helps learners:

- Understand Tacit Knowledge engineering principles
- Study organizational knowledge capture and reuse
- Learn engineering approaches to preserving experience
- Analyze knowledge transfer and collaboration challenges
- Evaluate decision-making using engineering expertise
- Explore emerging knowledge-centric technologies
