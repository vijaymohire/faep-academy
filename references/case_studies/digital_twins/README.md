# Digital Twins Case Studies

This collection provides curated case studies demonstrating the practical application of Digital Twins across engineering systems, manufacturing, healthcare, smart cities, industrial automation, aerospace, energy, enterprise operations, and intelligent infrastructure.

These case studies complement the FAEP Academy courses, tutorials, laboratories, notebooks, and engineering projects.

---

# Objectives

This collection helps learners:

- Understand Digital Twin architectures
- Study engineering integration strategies
- Learn from real-world Digital Twin deployments
- Analyze engineering and operational challenges
- Evaluate implementation trade-offs
- Explore emerging Digital Twin technologies
