# Space Systems Case Studies

This collection provides curated case studies demonstrating the practical application of Space Systems across satellite engineering, spacecraft design, mission operations, Earth observation, communications, navigation, autonomous systems, scientific exploration, and commercial space initiatives.

These case studies complement the FAEP Academy courses, tutorials, laboratories, notebooks, and engineering projects.

---

# Objectives

This collection helps learners:

- Understand Space Systems engineering architectures
- Study complete space mission lifecycles
- Learn engineering approaches to spacecraft and satellite systems
- Analyze mission planning and operational challenges
- Evaluate reliability, resilience, and mission assurance
- Explore emerging space technologies
