# Enterprise Engineering Case Studies

This collection provides curated case studies demonstrating the practical application of Enterprise Engineering across digital transformation, enterprise architecture, business modernization, cloud adoption, systems integration, governance, and intelligent enterprise operations.

These case studies complement the FAEP Academy courses, tutorials, laboratories, notebooks, and engineering projects.

---

# Objectives

This collection helps learners:

- Understand enterprise transformation initiatives
- Study enterprise architecture and systems integration
- Learn engineering approaches to digital modernization
- Analyze organizational and technical challenges
- Evaluate architecture and governance trade-offs
- Explore emerging enterprise technologies
