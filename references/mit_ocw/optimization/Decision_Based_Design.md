# Purpose

Decision-Based Design provides a systematic engineering methodology for selecting the best design alternatives while balancing performance, cost, risk, sustainability and mission objectives.

Within the QAI Computing Foundations this expands into an autonomous decision framework capable of supporting intelligent system design, digital engineering, world models, digital twins and multi-agent collaboration.

The objective is to transform engineering design from a static optimization activity into a continuous adaptive decision process.
