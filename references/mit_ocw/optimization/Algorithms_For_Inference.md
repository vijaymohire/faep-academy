# Purpose

Algorithms for Inference provide the computational foundation for reasoning under uncertainty, estimating hidden variables, making predictions and supporting autonomous decision making.

Within the QAI Computing Foundations this extends beyond probabilistic inference to support semantic reasoning, world models, digital twins, scientific discovery, autonomous agents and hybrid quantum-classical reasoning.

The objective is to develop scalable inference frameworks capable of integrating heterogeneous information sources while minimizing computation, communication and energy consumption.
