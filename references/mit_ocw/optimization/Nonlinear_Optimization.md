# Purpose

Nonlinear Optimization provides the mathematical and computational foundation for solving complex decision-making problems involving nonlinear objectives, constraints and dynamic environments.

Within the QAI Computing Foundations it enables adaptive optimization for world models, digital twins, autonomous systems, scientific computing and hybrid quantum-classical platforms.

The objective is to develop scalable optimization frameworks capable of minimizing computation, communication, energy consumption and mission cost while maximizing performance, robustness and autonomous decision making.
