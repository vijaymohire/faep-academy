# Purpose

Photonics studies the generation, transmission, manipulation and detection of light for communication, sensing and computation.

Within the QAI Computing Foundations this extends beyond optical communication to support photonic computing, quantum communication, optical AI accelerators, silicon photonics, quantum datacenters and hybrid classical-quantum computing infrastructures.

The objective is to develop scalable photonic architectures capable of enabling ultra-high bandwidth, low latency and energy-efficient computation across distributed QAI platforms.
