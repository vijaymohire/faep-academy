# Purpose

Quantum Communication studies how quantum information is generated, transmitted, protected and processed across distributed systems.

Within the QAI Computing Foundations this extends beyond communication channels to support distributed quantum computing, quantum fabrics, quantum data synchronization, hybrid classical-quantum systems and federated quantum intelligence.

The objective is to develop scalable communication architectures that integrate quantum and classical networks while supporting reliable, secure and efficient information exchange.
