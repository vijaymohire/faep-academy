# Purpose

Quantum Field Theory (QFT) provides the mathematical framework describing quantum particles as excitations of continuous fields governed by symmetry, interactions and conservation laws.

Within the QAI Computing Foundations this extends beyond particle physics to establish mathematical abstractions for hierarchical knowledge representation, world models, digital twins, semantic reasoning and distributed quantum intelligence.

The objective is to develop scalable computational frameworks that leverage field-theoretic principles to model interactions across hybrid classical-quantum systems.
