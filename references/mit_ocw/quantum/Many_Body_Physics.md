# Purpose

Many-Body Physics studies how large collections of interacting quantum systems produce emergent behavior that cannot be understood by analyzing individual particles alone.

Within the QAI Computing Foundations this extends into hierarchical world models, distributed quantum intelligence, tensor network computation, scientific AI, digital twins and hybrid classical-quantum ecosystems.

The objective is to develop scalable computational frameworks capable of modeling large interconnected systems through hierarchical abstractions and efficient tensor representations.
