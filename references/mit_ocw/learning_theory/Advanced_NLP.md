# Purpose

Advanced Natural Language Processing provides the foundation for enabling intelligent systems to understand, interpret, generate and reason using human language.

Within the QAI Computing Foundations this expands beyond traditional NLP to support semantic reasoning, knowledge graphs, world models, scientific AI, autonomous agents, digital twins and multi-agent communication.

The objective is to develop scalable language intelligence capable of integrating structured and unstructured knowledge across hybrid classical and quantum computing environments.
