# Purpose

Statistical Learning provides the mathematical and theoretical foundation for understanding how intelligent systems learn from data, generalize knowledge and make predictions.

Within the QAI Computing Foundations it supports adaptive learning, uncertainty estimation, world model evolution, digital twins, scientific AI and autonomous decision making.

The objective is to develop statistically robust AI systems capable of learning efficiently while reducing computation, communication and memory requirements across distributed hybrid classical and quantum platforms.
