# Purpose

Robotic Manipulation studies how intelligent robotic systems perceive, grasp, manipulate and interact with objects and environments.

Within the QAI Computing Foundations this extends into autonomous manipulation using world models, digital twins, semantic reasoning, multi-agent coordination and hybrid quantum-classical optimization.

The objective is to develop intelligent manipulation systems capable of operating safely, efficiently and autonomously across industrial, service, healthcare, space and scientific environments.
