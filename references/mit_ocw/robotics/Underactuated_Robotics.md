# Purpose

Underactuated Robotics studies robotic systems that possess fewer actuators than degrees of freedom, requiring intelligent control, planning and dynamic interaction with the environment.

Within the QAI Computing Foundations this expands into autonomous cyber-physical systems capable of adaptive perception, reasoning, planning and mission execution across terrestrial, aerial, underwater, orbital and extraterrestrial environments.

The objective is to develop intelligent robotic systems that combine learning, optimization, digital twins, world models and hybrid quantum-classical computing to operate efficiently in uncertain environments.
