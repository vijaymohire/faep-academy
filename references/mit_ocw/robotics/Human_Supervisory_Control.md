# Purpose

Human Supervisory Control studies how humans interact with autonomous systems to supervise, monitor, coordinate and intervene in complex missions.

Within the QAI Computing Foundations this expands into collaborative intelligence where humans, AI agents, robots, digital twins and world models operate together as coordinated decision-making systems.

The objective is to develop trustworthy supervisory frameworks that balance autonomy with human oversight while supporting scalable multi-agent ecosystems.
