# Purpose

Matrix methods provide the mathematical foundation for modern Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Scientific Computing and Quantum Computing.

Within the QAI Computing Foundations they become the basis for representing high-dimensional information, semantic knowledge, world models, digital twins and distributed intelligence.

The objective is not simply to perform matrix operations, but to derive scalable mathematical representations that reduce computation, memory movement and communication across hybrid classical and quantum platforms.
