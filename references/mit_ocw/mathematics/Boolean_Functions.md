# Purpose

Boolean functions provide the mathematical foundation for logical reasoning, decision making, constraint satisfaction, digital circuit design and computational logic.

Within the QAI Computing Foundations, Boolean functions extend beyond traditional binary logic to support intelligent reasoning, knowledge representation, autonomous planning, rule-based systems and hybrid quantum-classical decision models.

The objective is to develop scalable logical reasoning frameworks capable of supporting large-scale autonomous systems, distributed intelligence and world model reasoning.
