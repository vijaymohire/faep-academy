# Purpose

Lie Groups provide the mathematical foundation for describing continuous transformations, symmetry and geometric structures.

Within the QAI Computing Foundations they support robotic motion, autonomous navigation, computer vision, digital twins, world models, scientific computing and quantum computing.

The objective is to develop geometry-aware AI systems capable of representing motion, orientation, transformations and physical interactions across distributed intelligent systems.
