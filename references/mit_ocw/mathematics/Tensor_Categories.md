# Purpose

Tensor Categories provide a mathematical framework for representing compositional structures, hierarchical relationships and interactions between complex systems.

Within the QAI Computing Foundations they support semantic knowledge representation, hierarchical world models, digital twins, multi-agent systems, scientific computing and hybrid quantum-classical architectures.

The objective is to develop scalable mathematical abstractions capable of representing large interconnected ecosystems while supporting efficient reasoning, learning and distributed execution.
