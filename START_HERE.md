# START HERE

Welcome to **FAEP Academy**.

Whether you are a student, researcher, engineer, architect, developer, or technology leader, this repository is designed to help you progressively build knowledge across Artificial Intelligence, Quantum Computing, Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI), Systems Engineering, Applied Research, and Enterprise Transformation.

The academy emphasizes **engineering-first learning**, combining theory, practical laboratories, applied research, and enterprise architecture into a unified learning experience.
