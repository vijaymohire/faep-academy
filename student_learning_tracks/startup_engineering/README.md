# FAEP Startup Engineering

## Overview

The **FAEP Startup Engineering Learning Track** prepares students to transform engineering ideas, research outcomes and innovative technologies into scalable products, platforms and technology ventures.

The learning track integrates engineering design, product development, systems engineering, innovation management, platform engineering and commercialization into a practical methodology for building technology-driven startups.

Within the FAEP Academy, Startup Engineering bridges research, engineering and entrepreneurship by enabling learners to convert technical innovations into real-world solutions that create scientific, industrial and societal value.

I like the inclusion of Startup Engineering because it completes the journey from learning to innovation.

Rather than making this a business-school curriculum, I would keep it as an engineering entrepreneurship track. The emphasis should be on taking engineering ideas, architectures and research outcomes and transforming them into deployable products, platforms and sustainable technology ventures.

This fits well with the rest of the FAEP Academy, especially your work on the QAI Platform, Product Foundry, Innovation Management and commercialization.

---

# Vision

Develop engineering entrepreneurs capable of designing, validating, building and scaling technology platforms that address complex real-world challenges through innovation and responsible engineering.

The long-term objective is to establish Startup Engineering as the commercialization pathway for research, intellectual property and advanced engineering within the FAEP ecosystem.
