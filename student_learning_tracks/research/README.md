# FAEP Research Engineering

## Overview

The **FAEP Research Engineering Learning Track** prepares students to conduct rigorous engineering research, validate new ideas and contribute to the advancement of Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI), systems engineering, digital engineering and intelligent enterprise platforms.

The learning track integrates scientific inquiry, engineering methodologies, experimentation, validation, technical communication and innovation into a structured research framework.

Within the FAEP Academy, Research Engineering transforms learners from consumers of engineering knowledge into contributors capable of developing new architectures, frameworks, algorithms, technologies and engineering practices.

I think this is one of the most important sections in the entire academy.

Up to this point, students have been learning engineering. The Research track is where they begin creating new engineering knowledge.

I would therefore position this as the bridge between the Student Learning Tracks and the future Research Intelligence and Innovation Campus sections.

---

# Vision

Develop research capabilities that enable learners to investigate emerging technologies, validate engineering concepts and contribute original knowledge to academia, industry and open engineering communities.

The long-term objective is to establish Research Engineering as the innovation engine supporting the evolution of the Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform (FAEP).
