# FAEP Intermediate Learning Track

## Overview

The **FAEP Intermediate Learning Track** builds upon the Foundations Learning Track by introducing multidisciplinary engineering concepts and practical system integration. Students expand their knowledge of software engineering, systems engineering, artificial intelligence, cloud computing and digital engineering while developing the skills required for more complex engineering projects.

Within the FAEP Academy, the Intermediate Learning Track prepares learners to transition from understanding individual technologies to designing integrated intelligent systems that support real-world applications.

This file is the bridge between the Foundations and Advanced tracks.

I would position the Intermediate Learning Track as the point where students transition from learning individual concepts to integrating technologies and engineering systems. They begin thinking like engineers rather than just learners.
---

# Vision

Develop engineering capabilities that enable learners to integrate multiple technologies into reliable, scalable and maintainable intelligent systems.

The long-term objective is to prepare students for advanced engineering disciplines, research activities and enterprise-scale solution development.
