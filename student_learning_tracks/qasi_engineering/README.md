# FAEP QASI Engineering

## Overview

The **FAEP Quantum Artificial Super Intelligence (QASI) Engineering Learning Track** prepares students to investigate long-term engineering approaches for designing, validating and governing future generations of highly capable intelligent systems that integrate artificial intelligence, quantum computing, systems engineering and enterprise-scale intelligent platforms.

The learning track focuses on multidisciplinary engineering, architecture, validation methodologies, governance and research practices required for progressively more capable intelligent systems while maintaining strong emphasis on safety, transparency and human oversight.

Within the FAEP Academy, QASI Engineering represents an advanced research discipline that extends the principles established in QAI Computing Foundations, FTQC Engineering and QAGI Engineering toward future intelligent ecosystem engineering.

I think this should be treated as the most advanced research track in the FAEP Academy.

One recommendation is to frame QASI (Quantum Artificial Super Intelligence) as an aspirational research and systems engineering discipline, rather than implying the existence of such systems today. The emphasis should be on engineering methodologies, architectures, governance, validation and long-term research challenges.

This also creates a clean progression:

FTQC Engineering → reliable quantum computing
QAGI Engineering → increasingly capable general intelligence systems
QASI Engineering → long-term engineering, governance and ecosystem research for future highly capable intelligent systems

---

# Vision

Develop engineering expertise that enables learners to investigate scalable architectures, validation methodologies and governance models for future intelligent systems capable of addressing complex scientific, engineering and societal challenges.

The long-term objective is to advance engineering methodologies for future intelligent platforms through rigorous research, interdisciplinary collaboration and responsible innovation.
