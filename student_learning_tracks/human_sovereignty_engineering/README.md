# FAEP Human Sovereignty Engineering

## Overview

The **FAEP Human Sovereignty Engineering Learning Track** prepares students to design intelligent systems that augment human capabilities while preserving meaningful human oversight, accountability and decision authority.

The learning track integrates artificial intelligence, systems engineering, human-centered design, enterprise governance, robotics, cybersecurity and digital engineering to develop intelligent platforms that support human decision making rather than replace it.

Within the FAEP Academy, Human Sovereignty Engineering establishes engineering principles for building trustworthy, transparent and human-centric intelligent ecosystems.

This is probably the most novel learning track in the FAEP Academy, so I'd recommend defining it carefully.

Rather than framing it as a social or political concept, position Human Sovereignty Engineering as an engineering discipline focused on designing intelligent systems that preserve meaningful human oversight, autonomy, accountability and decision authority. That fits naturally with your existing work on governance, systems engineering, AI, robotics and federated architectures.

---

# Vision

Develop engineering expertise that enables learners to design intelligent systems that maximize human potential through collaboration between people, artificial intelligence and autonomous technologies.

The long-term objective is to establish Human Sovereignty Engineering as a foundational discipline for responsible intelligent systems that maintain human agency throughout the system lifecycle.
