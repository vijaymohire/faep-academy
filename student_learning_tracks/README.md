# FAEP Student Learning Tracks

## Overview

The **FAEP Student Learning Tracks** provide a structured educational pathway that guides students, engineers, researchers and innovators from fundamental engineering principles to advanced Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI), Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform (FAEP) engineering and emerging intelligent systems.

The learning tracks integrate the Mathematical Foundations, QAI Computing Foundations, FAEP Reference Architecture, Engineering Frameworks, Engineering Patterns, Technology Libraries and Standards Library into a progressive educational curriculum.

Within the FAEP Academy, each learning track combines theory, laboratories, projects and research activities to prepare learners for academic research, industrial engineering and entrepreneurial innovation.

---

# Vision

Develop a comprehensive engineering curriculum that enables learners to progress from foundational knowledge to advanced intelligent systems engineering while maintaining strong links between theory, architecture, implementation and innovation.

The long-term objective is to establish the FAEP Academy as a global educational platform for Quantum AI, autonomous systems and enterprise engineering.
