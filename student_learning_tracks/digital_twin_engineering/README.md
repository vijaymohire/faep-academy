# FAEP Digital Twin Engineering

## Overview

The **FAEP Digital Twin Engineering Learning Track** prepares students to design, develop and manage intelligent digital twins that represent physical systems, cyber systems, enterprise processes and autonomous ecosystems.

The learning track integrates systems engineering, artificial intelligence, simulation, robotics, cloud computing, semantic technologies and enterprise architecture to build digital twins capable of supporting monitoring, prediction, optimization and autonomous decision making.

Within the FAEP Academy, Digital Twin Engineering serves as a multidisciplinary engineering discipline connecting the physical and digital worlds through intelligent models and continuous data integration.

# Vision

Develop engineering expertise that enables learners to create scalable, interoperable and intelligent digital twin solutions supporting scientific research, industrial automation, enterprise transformation and autonomous ecosystems.

The long-term objective is to establish Digital Twin Engineering as a foundational discipline for future intelligent enterprises and cyber-physical systems.
