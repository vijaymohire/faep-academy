# FAEP Ecosystem Engineering

## Overview

The **FAEP Ecosystem Engineering Learning Track** prepares students to design, integrate and govern large-scale intelligent ecosystems composed of interconnected people, organizations, platforms, intelligent agents, digital twins and autonomous systems.

Unlike traditional engineering disciplines that focus on individual systems, Ecosystem Engineering emphasizes the coordinated development of multiple interacting systems operating within a federated architecture.

Within the FAEP Academy, this learning track integrates systems engineering, enterprise architecture, artificial intelligence, digital engineering, cloud computing, robotics and quantum computing into a unified engineering methodology for intelligent ecosystems.

This is one of the most unique learning tracks in your repository because FAEP Ecosystem Engineering is not about a single technology or discipline—it is about engineering an entire federated intelligent ecosystem.
