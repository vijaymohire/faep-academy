# FAEP Research Intelligence

## Overview

The **FAEP Research Intelligence** framework transforms scientific knowledge, engineering research and technological discoveries into structured intelligence that supports innovation, engineering excellence and enterprise decision making.

The framework integrates knowledge acquisition, literature analysis, engineering research, experimental validation, technology assessment and innovation management into a unified research intelligence ecosystem.

Within the FAEP Academy, Research Intelligence serves as the knowledge acceleration layer connecting research, engineering, innovation and technology commercialization.

One suggestion: make a distinction between Research Engineering (what students do) and Research Intelligence (how the FAEP ecosystem captures, organizes, synthesizes and accelerates engineering knowledge). That makes Research Intelligence a higher-level capability rather than another research course.

I would position it as the knowledge acceleration layer that sits between research and innovation.
---

# Vision

Develop a comprehensive research intelligence platform capable of continuously discovering, organizing, validating and transforming engineering knowledge into practical technologies, reusable engineering assets and innovative products.

The long-term objective is to establish Research Intelligence as the strategic knowledge engine supporting the evolution of the Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform (FAEP).
