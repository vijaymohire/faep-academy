# Overview

The **Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI)** repository provides a comprehensive educational framework for understanding, designing, engineering, validating, and deploying Quantum Artificial Intelligence systems within the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

QAI combines the strengths of classical Artificial Intelligence (AI), Quantum Computing (QC), advanced optimization, Digital Twins, Knowledge Graphs, enterprise engineering, and intelligent automation to solve increasingly complex scientific and enterprise challenges.

Rather than replacing classical AI, QAI extends existing technologies by integrating hybrid quantum-classical computing architectures that progressively mature alongside advances in quantum hardware.
