# QAI Optimization Algorithms

## Overview

The **QAI Optimization Algorithms** library provides the mathematical and computational foundation for solving optimization problems across Artificial Intelligence, Quantum Computing, Robotics, Scientific Computing and Enterprise Systems.

Optimization lies at the heart of intelligent decision-making, enabling systems to efficiently allocate resources, improve performance, minimize cost and maximize desired outcomes.

Within the FAEP ecosystem, optimization algorithms support planning, scheduling, mission execution, resource orchestration and autonomous decision-making.

---

# Vision

Develop a reusable library of optimization algorithms that can operate across classical, AI and quantum computing environments.

The long-term objective is to build hybrid optimization engines capable of supporting Digital Twins, Multi-Agent Systems, Scientific Discovery and Enterprise Transformation.
