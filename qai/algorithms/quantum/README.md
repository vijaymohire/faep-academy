# QAI Quantum Algorithms

## Overview

The **QAI Quantum Algorithms** library provides the computational foundation for quantum-enhanced computing within the FAEP ecosystem.

Quantum algorithms enable intelligent systems to solve classes of computational problems that are difficult or impractical for classical computing alone. These include optimization, simulation, machine learning, cryptography and scientific discovery.

Within the QAI Computing Foundations, quantum algorithms are integrated with artificial intelligence, digital twins, world models, robotics and enterprise systems to create hybrid classical-quantum computing platforms.

---

# Vision

Develop a reusable quantum algorithm library supporting hybrid quantum-classical computing across enterprise systems, scientific computing, robotics and cyber-physical ecosystems.

The long-term objective is to establish QAI as an engineering discipline that combines quantum computation, artificial intelligence and systems engineering into deployable platform capabilities.
