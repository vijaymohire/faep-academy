# QAI Digital Twin Data Structures

## Overview

The **QAI Digital Twin Data Structures** library provides the foundational representation for creating synchronized digital representations of physical, virtual and cyber-physical systems within the FAEP ecosystem.

Digital twin data structures enable intelligent systems to continuously model, monitor, simulate and optimize assets throughout their lifecycle by maintaining an evolving relationship between the physical and digital worlds.

Within the QAI Computing Foundations, digital twins integrate semantic knowledge, knowledge graphs, world models, autonomous agents and hybrid classical-quantum computing into reusable engineering assets.

---

# Vision

Develop a reusable digital twin library capable of representing assets, processes, organizations and intelligent ecosystems across manufacturing, enterprise systems, robotics, scientific computing and smart infrastructure.

The long-term objective is to establish digital twins as the operational intelligence layer connecting real-world systems with autonomous decision-making.
