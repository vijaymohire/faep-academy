# QAI Quantum Data Structures

## Overview

The **QAI Quantum Data Structures** library provides the foundational representation for quantum information, quantum computation and hybrid classical-quantum systems within the FAEP ecosystem.

Quantum data structures organize quantum states, logical qubits, entanglement relationships and quantum computational resources, enabling scalable quantum algorithms and fault-tolerant quantum computing.

Within the QAI Computing Foundations, quantum data structures integrate quantum information science with semantic knowledge, digital twins, world models and artificial intelligence to support next-generation QAI platforms.

---

# Vision

Develop a reusable quantum data structure library capable of representing quantum information across scientific computing, enterprise platforms, robotics and hybrid classical-quantum systems.

The long-term objective is to establish standardized quantum knowledge representations that enable interoperability between quantum hardware, runtimes and intelligent software platforms.
