# FAEP Quantum Technologies

## Overview

The **FAEP Quantum Technology Library** provides a vendor-neutral reference to quantum computing technologies, quantum information science and hybrid classical-quantum platforms used within the Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform (FAEP).

The library covers quantum hardware, quantum software, quantum algorithms, quantum networking, quantum error correction, hybrid computing and enterprise quantum technologies that support artificial intelligence, digital twins, scientific computing and next-generation intelligent systems.

Within the QAI Computing Foundations, quantum technologies extend classical computing with quantum capabilities to solve computationally intensive problems and accelerate scientific discovery.

This is probably the flagship technology library of the FAEP Academy because quantum computing has been one of the central themes throughout your work.

Unlike many quantum introductions, this should not only explain quantum technologies but also show how they integrate with AI, cloud, robotics and enterprise systems. That makes it consistent with your vision of QAI (Quantum Artificial Intelligence) rather than treating quantum as an isolated discipline.

---

# Vision

Develop a comprehensive quantum technology reference that enables students, engineers and researchers to understand, evaluate and integrate quantum technologies while maintaining technology-independent engineering principles.

The long-term objective is to establish a reusable quantum technology catalog supporting research, enterprise innovation and hybrid classical-quantum intelligent ecosystems.
