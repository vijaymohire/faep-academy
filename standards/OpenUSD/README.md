# FAEP OpenUSD Standards

## Overview

The **FAEP OpenUSD Standards Library** provides a structured reference to OpenUSD (Open Universal Scene Description), open 3D scene representation, digital asset interoperability and collaborative virtual engineering technologies relevant to the Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform (FAEP).

OpenUSD enables standardized representation, composition and exchange of complex 3D scenes, digital assets and simulation environments across engineering, robotics, manufacturing, artificial intelligence and digital twin ecosystems.

Within the FAEP Academy, OpenUSD complements the QAI Computing Foundations, FAEP Reference Architecture, Engineering Frameworks, Engineering Patterns and Technology Libraries by providing a common representation for digital engineering and virtual environments.

OpenUSD is a great addition because it complements your work on Digital Twins, World Models, Simulation, Robotics, AI, and Knowledge Graphs.

Unlike OpenTelemetry (observability), OpenUSD is about interoperable 3D scene description and digital asset exchange. I would position it as the digital engineering and virtual world representation standard within the FAEP Academy.

This also aligns with your long-term vision of enterprise digital twins, simulation environments, and intelligent world models.
