# FAEP NIST Standards & Frameworks

## Overview

The **FAEP NIST Standards & Frameworks Library** provides a structured reference to cybersecurity guidance, risk management frameworks, artificial intelligence governance, measurement science and technology best practices developed by the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST).

NIST publications support secure, trustworthy and resilient engineering across cloud computing, artificial intelligence, software engineering, critical infrastructure, digital engineering and emerging technologies.

Within the FAEP Academy, NIST frameworks complement the QAI Computing Foundations, FAEP Reference Architecture, Engineering Frameworks, Engineering Patterns and Technology Libraries by providing practical guidance for cybersecurity, privacy, AI governance and enterprise risk management.

NIST is another cornerstone of the Standards Library, but unlike ISO or IEEE, I would position it as the reference framework organization for cybersecurity, AI risk management, privacy, cloud security and measurement science.

The FAEP NIST Standards & Frameworks Library establishes the security and trust reference for the Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform.

# Contributing

When adding a new NIST publication or framework:

* Describe its purpose and scope.
* Explain its relevance to the FAEP architecture.
* Reference related engineering frameworks, patterns and technologies.
* Identify security, privacy and risk management considerations.
* Describe relationships with ISO, IEEE, INCOSE and other standards where applicable.
* Promote secure engineering and technology neutrality.
* Reference official NIST publications.
* Keep content current with new framework revisions and special publications.
