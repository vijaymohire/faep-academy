# Book Template

> **FAEP Education | Standard Template for Technical Books, Handbooks, and Bodies of Knowledge**

---

# Overview

This template provides a standardized structure for developing books, technical handbooks, practitioner guides, reference manuals, and Bodies of Knowledge (BoK) across the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

The objective is to produce modular, reusable, technically accurate publications that integrate educational content, engineering practices, enterprise architecture, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI), and systems engineering.

Books developed using this template are intended to support education, research, enterprise adoption, professional development, and future certification programs.

# AI Authoring Guidelines

When generating book content:

- Follow this template consistently.
- Maintain a professional and educational tone.
- Explain concepts before implementation.
- Use modular chapters.
- Reuse existing educational assets.
- Avoid proprietary algorithms or confidential information.
- Reference public repositories where appropriate.
- Encourage further exploration through tutorials and labs.
- Ensure consistency across chapters.

Books should be engineered as living knowledge assets rather than static documents.

# FAEP Perspective

Within FAEP, books are treated as curated knowledge assets built upon reusable educational resources rather than isolated publications.

By integrating repositories, tutorials, laboratories, case studies, references, and assessments, books provide structured learning pathways that support education, enterprise adoption, professional development, and future certification.

This modular approach also enables the creation of comprehensive references such as the future **FAEP Quantum Artificial Intelligence Body of Knowledge (QAI-BoK)** while preserving a clear separation between public educational material and proprietary implementation details.
