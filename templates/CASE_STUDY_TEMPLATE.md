# Overview

This template provides a standardized structure for documenting engineering, enterprise, scientific, and research case studies across the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

Case studies demonstrate how real or representative challenges can be addressed using systems engineering, enterprise architecture, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI), Digital Twins, Knowledge Graphs, Hybrid Runtime, and related FAEP technologies.

The objective is to capture practical experience, engineering decisions, lessons learned, and reusable knowledge that can benefit learners, researchers, and enterprise practitioners.

# AI Authoring Guidelines

When creating Case Studies:

- Focus on engineering decisions rather than marketing.
- Explain the problem before presenting the solution.
- Include measurable outcomes where possible.
- Discuss trade-offs and lessons learned.
- Encourage critical thinking.
- Reference public FAEP repositories where appropriate.
- Avoid proprietary algorithms, confidential information, or customer-sensitive details.

---

# FAEP Perspective

Within FAEP, case studies transform engineering experience into reusable knowledge assets.

Rather than simply documenting successful outcomes, they explain the reasoning, architecture, implementation strategy, validation approach, and lessons learned behind each solution. This helps learners understand not only **what** was built, but also **why** specific engineering decisions were made and how those decisions contributed to enterprise success.

Case studies also provide a bridge between theory, practical laboratories, enterprise projects, and future innovation across the FAEP ecosystem.
