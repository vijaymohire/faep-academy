# FAEP Academy Documentation Templates

## Overview

The **templates** directory contains the standard documentation templates used throughout the FAEP Academy repository.

These templates provide a consistent engineering methodology for documenting knowledge assets, ensuring that research, education, engineering, and product development follow a common structure.

The objective is to transform academic knowledge into reusable engineering assets that support the **QAI Computing Foundations**, the **Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform (FAEP)**, and future commercial platforms.

---

# Design Philosophy

The templates follow a common engineering lifecycle.

```text
Knowledge Source
        ↓
Knowledge Extraction
        ↓
Engineering Principles
        ↓
QAI Knowledge Extraction
        ↓
FAEP Mapping
        ↓
Platform Assets
        ↓
Implementation
        ↓
Validation
        ↓
Research
        ↓
Student Learning
```

This methodology enables knowledge from universities, industry standards, research papers and internal innovation to be transformed into deployable engineering solutions.
