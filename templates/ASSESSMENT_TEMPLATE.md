# Overview

This template provides a standardized structure for creating assessments across the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

Assessments measure learner understanding, practical engineering skills, problem-solving ability, and competency in applying Artificial Intelligence (AI), Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI), enterprise architecture, systems engineering, and related technologies.

The template supports academic learning, professional development, certification preparation, and enterprise workforce assessment.

# AI Authoring Guidelines

When creating assessments:

- Align with learning objectives.
- Evaluate understanding and practical competency.
- Prefer realistic engineering scenarios.
- Encourage critical thinking.
- Assess systems integration rather than isolated facts.
- Promote enterprise engineering best practices.
- Reference public FAEP repositories where appropriate.
- Avoid proprietary implementations or confidential information.
- Provide clear evaluation criteria and expected outcomes.

# FAEP Perspective

Within FAEP, assessments are designed to measure more than theoretical knowledge.

They evaluate a learner's ability to apply engineering principles, integrate multidisciplinary technologies, solve realistic enterprise problems, and demonstrate competency across Artificial Intelligence, Quantum Artificial Intelligence, enterprise architecture, systems engineering, Digital Twins, and intelligent enterprise ecosystems.

Assessments emphasize continuous learning, practical application, collaboration, innovation, and professional engineering practice.
