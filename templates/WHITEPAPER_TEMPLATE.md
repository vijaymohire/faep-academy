# Overview

Explain:

- Background
- Motivation

- Engineering context
- Scope

Whitepapers are where FAEP presents its vision, architecture, research direction, and engineering philosophy to the outside world.

A whitepaper is not a research paper. I would differentiate them as:

| Document       | Purpose                                                                               |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Tutorial       | Teach                                                                                 |
| Reference      | Explain                                                                               |
| Lab            | Practice                                                                              |
| Case Study     | Demonstrate                                                                           |
| Research Paper | Present novel scientific results                                                      |
| **Whitepaper** | Present vision, architecture, engineering approach, roadmap, and industry perspective |

Most of your repositories (QAI Hub, QAI Cloud, FAEP, QAI Architecture, Enterprise Intelligence) will eventually have a corresponding whitepaper.

# AI Authoring Guidelines

When creating whitepapers:

- Present a clear vision before technical details.
- Support architectural decisions with engineering rationale.
- Maintain an objective and professional tone.
- Distinguish current capabilities from future goals.
- Avoid proprietary algorithms or confidential information.
- Reference public repositories and standards where appropriate.
- Use diagrams and tables to improve clarity.
- Keep terminology consistent across the FAEP ecosystem.

# FAEP Perspective

Within FAEP, whitepapers communicate strategic vision, architectural thinking, engineering methodology, and long-term technology direction. They bridge research, enterprise architecture, systems engineering, and practical implementation by presenting reusable concepts rather than product-specific details.

Whitepapers are intended to guide education, research, enterprise transformation, and collaboration while remaining technology-aware, standards-informed, and adaptable as the ecosystem evolves.

They also provide the conceptual foundation for future publications, including the FAEP Quantum Artificial Intelligence Body of Knowledge (QAI-BoK), technical books, and reference architectures.
