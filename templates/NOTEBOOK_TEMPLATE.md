# Notebook Template

> **FAEP Education | Standard Template for Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, and Quantum Artificial Intelligence Notebooks**

---

# Overview

This template provides a standardized structure for developing interactive notebooks across the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

Notebooks combine theory, architecture, mathematics, code, experimentation, visualization, and engineering validation into a single interactive learning resource.

Unlike static documentation, notebooks allow learners to explore concepts, execute code, analyze results, and iteratively improve their understanding through experimentation.

# AI Authoring Guidelines

When creating notebooks:

- Explain concepts before code.
- Keep notebooks modular.
- Encourage experimentation.
- Include reproducible examples.
- Use public datasets where possible.
- Avoid proprietary algorithms or confidential information.
- Document assumptions and limitations.
- Include validation and interpretation of results.

# FAEP Perspective

Within FAEP, notebooks are interactive engineering assets rather than simple executable documents.

They combine theory, mathematics, experimentation, architecture, visualization, and engineering validation into a reproducible learning experience that supports Artificial Intelligence, Quantum Artificial Intelligence, enterprise architecture, scientific research, and multidisciplinary engineering.

Well-designed notebooks encourage exploration, experimentation, collaboration, and continuous learning while serving as reusable knowledge assets across the FAEP ecosystem.
