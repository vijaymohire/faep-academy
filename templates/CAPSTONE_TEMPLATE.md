# Capstone Project Template

# Overview

This template provides a standardized structure for developing Capstone Projects across the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

Capstone Projects represent the culmination of a learning pathway by integrating knowledge from multiple repositories, tutorials, laboratories, case studies, and engineering disciplines into a complete, enterprise-scale solution.

The objective is to demonstrate practical engineering competency, systems thinking, innovation, and multidisciplinary collaboration while solving realistic business, scientific, or mission-oriented problems.

# AI Authoring Guidelines

When creating Capstone Projects:

- Focus on realistic enterprise or research scenarios.
- Encourage multidisciplinary integration.
- Align with learning objectives.
- Promote reusable architectures and frameworks.
- Avoid proprietary algorithms or confidential information.
- Reference public FAEP repositories where appropriate.
- Include measurable success criteria and validation activities.

---

# FAEP Perspective

Within FAEP, Capstone Projects represent the highest level of practical learning. They demonstrate a learner's ability to integrate enterprise architecture, systems engineering, Artificial Intelligence, Quantum Artificial Intelligence, Digital Twins, cloud computing, and related technologies into cohesive, enterprise-ready solutions.

Successful capstone projects may evolve into reusable reference architectures, accelerator kits, case studies, or innovation initiatives, contributing back to the broader FAEP ecosystem.
