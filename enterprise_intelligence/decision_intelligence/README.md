# Overview

The **Decision Intelligence** repository provides a comprehensive framework for understanding, designing, engineering, and governing intelligent decision-making systems within the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

Decision Intelligence combines Artificial Intelligence (AI), Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI), Knowledge Graphs, Digital Twins, Enterprise Reasoning, predictive analytics, optimization, and human expertise to support informed, explainable, and evidence-based decisions across enterprise operations.

Rather than replacing human judgement, Decision Intelligence augments decision-makers by providing timely insights, recommendations, scenario analysis, and optimization while maintaining transparency and governance.

This is another foundational repository because it sits at the heart of Enterprise Intelligence.

Looking across everything we've built, I think the Enterprise Intelligence repositories now have a very clear separation of concerns:

| Module                    | Primary Question                            |
| ------------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| Enterprise Intelligence   | How does the enterprise become intelligent? |
| Agentic AI                | Who performs intelligent actions?           |
| Automation                | How are repeatable tasks executed?          |

| Enterprise Memory         | What knowledge do we remember?              |
| World Models              | How do we understand reality?               |
| Enterprise Copilots       | How do we assist users?                     |

This makes Decision Intelligence the bridge between reasoning and execution.
