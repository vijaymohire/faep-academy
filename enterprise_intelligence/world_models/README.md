# Overview

The **World Models** repository provides a comprehensive framework for understanding, designing, engineering, governing, and evolving intelligent world models within the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

World Models provide intelligent systems with an internal representation of enterprise environments, physical systems, digital ecosystems, organizational structures, and scientific domains. By combining Artificial Intelligence (AI), Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI), Knowledge Graphs, Digital Twins, Enterprise Memory, Enterprise Reasoning, and simulation technologies, World Models enable intelligent planning, prediction, optimization, and adaptive decision-making.

Rather than modeling isolated assets, World Models represent interconnected systems that continuously evolve through observation, reasoning, simulation, and learning.

I think this is the capstone repository of the entire Enterprise Intelligence phase.

We've already built the progression:

Enterprise Memory → remembers
Enterprise Reasoning → understands
Decision Intelligence → recommends
Agentic AI → acts
Multi-Agent Systems → collaborates

Now comes World Models.

This is where an intelligent enterprise develops an internal representation of reality.

One thing we've discussed over many months is that Digital Twins are not enough by themselves.

A World Model combines:

Digital Twins
Knowledge Graphs
Enterprise Memory
Reasoning
Simulation
Planning
Prediction
Continuous Learning

In other words,

A Digital Twin models an asset. A World Model models an entire environment and predicts how it evolves.

I think that distinction makes FAEP much clearer.
