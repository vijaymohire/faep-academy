# Overview

The **Automation** repository provides a comprehensive framework for understanding, designing, engineering, governing, and deploying intelligent automation across enterprise environments within the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

Automation combines Artificial Intelligence (AI), Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI), Digital Twins, Knowledge Graphs, enterprise engineering, workflow orchestration, and intelligent agents to automate repetitive, predictable, and data-driven activities while maintaining governance, transparency, and human oversight.

Rather than replacing people, enterprise automation removes repetitive operational work, allowing organizations to focus on innovation, decision-making, research, engineering, and customer value.

This is the natural complement to Augmentation.

I think the distinction within the Enterprise Intelligence stack should now be very clear:

| Repository                | Primary Purpose                                 |
| ------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| **Agentic AI**            | Intelligent planning and autonomous action      |
| **Automation**            | Repeatable execution of processes and workflows |
| **Augmentation**          | Enhancing human capabilities                    |
| **Decision Intelligence** | Supporting better decisions                     |
| **Enterprise Copilots**   | Context-aware human assistance                  |

Automation is therefore not just RPA (Robotic Process Automation). In the FAEP ecosystem, it spans the entire enterprise—from engineering pipelines to intelligent manufacturing, cloud operations, AI operations, quantum workflows, and business processes.
