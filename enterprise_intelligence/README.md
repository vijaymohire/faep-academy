# Overview

The **Enterprise Intelligence** repository provides a comprehensive framework for understanding how modern enterprises transform data, information, knowledge, and engineering assets into intelligent decision-making, automation, augmentation, and continuous organizational learning within the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

Enterprise Intelligence combines Artificial Intelligence (AI), Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI), Knowledge Graphs, Digital Twins, enterprise engineering, decision intelligence, reasoning systems, automation, and human collaboration into a unified operational framework.

Rather than focusing on individual AI models, Enterprise Intelligence establishes an enterprise-wide intelligence layer that supports research, engineering, products, commercialization, modernization, governance, and strategic planning.

This repository is actually the bridge between the engineering work we've completed and the educational content we'll build next.

If we look at the overall FAEP architecture now, it naturally becomes:

Engineering Foundations
        │
Knowledge Assets
        │
Enterprise Intelligence
        │
Learning Resources
        │
Enterprise Vision

Enterprise Intelligence is where knowledge becomes actionable intelligence through AI, QAI, reasoning, planning, decision support, automation, and augmentation.

Rather than simply describing AI technologies, this repository should explain how an intelligent enterprise operates.
