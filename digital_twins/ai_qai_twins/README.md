# Overview

The **AI & QAI Twins** repository focuses on designing, managing, synchronizing, governing, and continuously improving Digital Twins for Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI) systems within the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

Unlike traditional Digital Twins that primarily represent physical assets, AI & QAI Twins represent intelligent digital entities including AI models, Quantum AI models, AI agents, enterprise copilots, autonomous systems, and hybrid quantum-classical intelligence.

These twins enable continuous monitoring, validation, explainability, lifecycle management, governance, and responsible deployment of intelligent systems.

Unlike traditional Digital Twins that model machines or products, AI & QAI Twins represent intelligent digital entities—AI models, agents, copilots, autonomous systems, and future Quantum AI systems. These twins can be monitored, validated, governed, versioned, and continuously improved throughout their lifecycle.
