# Overview

The **Product Twins** repository provides a comprehensive framework for designing, developing, synchronizing, governing, and continuously improving Product Digital Twins within the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

A Product Twin is a continuously synchronized digital representation of a product throughout its entire lifecycle, including research, design, engineering, manufacturing, deployment, operations, maintenance, modernization, and retirement.

By integrating Digital Twins, Knowledge Graphs, Enterprise Ontologies, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI), engineering data, operational telemetry, and lifecycle management, Product Twins provide a complete view of product evolution and performance.

I actually think Product Twins are one of the most commercially valuable Digital Twins because they directly connect engineering, manufacturing, operations, services, and customers.

Given all the work we've done around the QAI Factory, Product Foundry, Technology Portfolio, Modernization, and TRL maturity, I would broaden Product Twins well beyond manufacturing.

I would define it as:

A continuously synchronized digital representation of a product throughout its complete lifecycle, from concept and research through engineering, production, deployment, operation, modernization, and retirement.

That aligns perfectly with the FAEP philosophy.
