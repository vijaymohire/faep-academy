# Overview

The **Enterprise Twins** repository provides a comprehensive framework for designing, developing, synchronizing, governing, and continuously improving Enterprise Digital Twins within the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

An Enterprise Twin is a continuously synchronized digital representation of an organization's strategy, business capabilities, governance, people, products, services, processes, technologies, knowledge assets, infrastructure, and operational state.

Enterprise Twins enable organizations to understand, simulate, optimize, modernize, and continuously improve enterprise operations by integrating Digital Twins, Knowledge Graphs, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI), enterprise architecture, and intelligent automation.


I actually think Enterprise Twins are the centerpiece of the entire Digital Twins repository.

Everything we've built recently—HEEA, EPCRM, FAEP, FAEF, QAITF, capability planes, business verticals, modernization framework, holding company architecture—naturally converges here.

Unlike most Enterprise Digital Twin definitions that focus on business processes or ERP data, ours is much broader.

I would define an Enterprise Twin as:

A continuously synchronized digital representation of an enterprise that integrates business strategy, governance, people, processes, products, technologies, knowledge, operations, and intelligent systems throughout their lifecycle.
