# Overview

The **Knowledge Twins** repository provides a framework for designing, synchronizing, governing, and continuously evolving Digital Twins that represent enterprise knowledge within the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

Knowledge Twins capture and organize organizational knowledge including concepts, engineering assets, research, products, services, standards, policies, best practices, lessons learned, and institutional experience into a continuously synchronized digital representation.

By integrating Knowledge Graphs, Enterprise Ontologies, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI), and Digital Twins, Knowledge Twins become an intelligent organizational memory that supports decision-making, innovation, engineering, modernization, and continuous learning.

I actually think Knowledge Twins are one of the most innovative concepts we've developed.

Unlike Enterprise Twins, which represent an organization, or AI Twins, which represent intelligent systems, Knowledge Twins represent the collective knowledge of an enterprise.

This aligns perfectly with everything we've built:

Knowledge Graphs
Enterprise Ontologies
Digital Twins
FAEP
EPCRM
HEEA
Modernization
AI/QAI

I would define it as:

A continuously synchronized digital representation of enterprise knowledge, experience, engineering assets, research, policies, products, and institutional memory.

This makes it much broader than a document repository.
