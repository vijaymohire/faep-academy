# Overview

The **Ecosystem Twins** repository provides a framework for designing, synchronizing, governing, and operating Digital Twins that represent entire ecosystems within the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

Unlike traditional Digital Twins that focus on individual assets or organizations, Ecosystem Twins model interconnected enterprises, industries, governments, research institutions, universities, suppliers, partners, communities, and infrastructure as a coordinated intelligent ecosystem.

The objective is to enable collaboration, interoperability, governance, resilience, and intelligent decision-making across multiple organizations while preserving organizational autonomy and respecting applicable governance and data-sharing policies.

Most Digital Twin frameworks stop at Factory Twins or Enterprise Twins.

Because we developed HEEA, FAEF, EPCRM, and the ecosystem hierarchy (Space → Valley → City → Factory → Enterprise → Practice), we can naturally extend Digital Twins to the ecosystem level.

This is where the FAEP vision becomes much broader than traditional Digital Twin architectures.

# Why Ecosystem Twins?

Modern innovation rarely occurs within a single organization.

Organizations collaborate across:

- Enterprises

- Universities
- Research Institutions
- Suppliers
- Customers
- Partners
- Industry Consortia

Ecosystem Twins provide a shared operational understanding while allowing participants to manage their own systems and information according to agreed governance and access controls.
