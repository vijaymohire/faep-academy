# Overview

The **Synchronization** repository provides a comprehensive framework for maintaining continuous consistency between real-world entities and their Digital Twins within the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

Synchronization enables Digital Twins to remain accurate, trusted, governed, and operational by continuously exchanging information between physical systems, enterprise platforms, Knowledge Graphs, AI systems, and Quantum AI services.

Rather than performing periodic updates, synchronization establishes continuous lifecycle alignment across enterprise operations, engineering systems, Digital Twins, and intelligent decision-support platforms.
