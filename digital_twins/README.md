# Overview

The **Digital Twins** repository provides a comprehensive framework for designing, developing, governing, and operating Digital Twins within the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

A Digital Twin is a continuously synchronized digital representation of a physical, digital, organizational, or conceptual entity. Digital Twins combine real-world data, models, simulations, Knowledge Graphs, Artificial Intelligence (AI), and Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI) to improve understanding, decision-making, automation, and enterprise modernization.

Rather than focusing only on industrial equipment, this repository adopts a broader enterprise perspective where Digital Twins can represent people, products, processes, enterprises, ecosystems, scientific models, and intelligent systems.

Looking at all the work we've done over the last few months, I'd position it not as a collection of Digital Twin examples, but as a Digital Twin Reference Architecture that spans humans, enterprises, products, infrastructure, AI, and future Quantum AI systems.
