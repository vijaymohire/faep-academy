# Overview

The **Infrastructure Twins** repository provides a comprehensive framework for designing, synchronizing, governing, and operating Infrastructure Digital Twins within the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

Infrastructure Twins provide continuously synchronized digital representations of physical and digital infrastructure including data centers, cloud platforms, networks, communication systems, computing resources, industrial facilities, utilities, and emerging Quantum AI infrastructure.

By combining Digital Twins, Knowledge Graphs, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI), real-time telemetry, and predictive analytics, Infrastructure Twins enable intelligent operations, resilience, optimization, and enterprise modernization.

This is another important repository because Infrastructure Twins become the foundation upon which all AI, QAI, Digital Twins, and enterprise services operate.

Most Digital Twin literature focuses on physical infrastructure. Within the FAEP ecosystem, I think we should broaden this to include physical, digital, cloud, quantum, networking, and intelligent infrastructure.
