# Organizational Intelligence

> Learn how organizations can develop intelligence through knowledge management, systems thinking, Artificial Intelligence, digital engineering, and Quantum AI (QAI).

---

# Overview

Organizational Intelligence (OI) is the capability of an organization to continuously learn, adapt, innovate, make informed decisions, and achieve strategic objectives by effectively integrating people, processes, knowledge, technology, and data.

Within the FAEP Academy, Organizational Intelligence extends beyond traditional management concepts by combining systems engineering, Artificial Intelligence, Digital Twins, Engineering Memory, Knowledge Graphs, and Quantum AI to support intelligent enterprises.

This learning path introduces the principles, architectures, and technologies required to design organizations that continuously improve through knowledge-driven decision making and adaptive engineering.
