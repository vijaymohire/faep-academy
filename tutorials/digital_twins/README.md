# Digital Twins

## Overview

Digital Twins are continuously evolving digital representations of physical, digital, logical, or conceptual entities that enable organizations to monitor, analyze, simulate, optimize, and manage systems throughout their lifecycle.

Originally developed for industrial engineering and aerospace applications, Digital Twins have expanded into manufacturing, healthcare, transportation, smart cities, scientific research, enterprise architecture, cybersecurity, Artificial Intelligence (AI), and increasingly Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI).

Within the FAEP Academy, Digital Twins are viewed as **Information Twins**—trusted information models that synchronize knowledge between the real world and intelligent digital systems.

Rather than serving as static models, Digital Twins continuously evolve using real-time information, simulations, analytics, Artificial Intelligence, and hybrid computing technologies.

# Digital Twin Control Loops and Observer Framework

## Overview

A Digital Twin is not simply a digital model of a physical system. It is part of a continuous information loop that observes, represents, analyzes, predicts, and sometimes influences the behavior of the system it represents.

Depending on the application, a Digital Twin may operate as an open-loop system, a human-assisted decision support system, or a fully autonomous closed-loop control system.

The FAEP Academy views these as progressive maturity levels that increase automation while maintaining engineering rigor, safety, and trust.

---

# Open-Loop Digital Twins

Open-loop Digital Twins observe systems without directly influencing them.

```text
Physical System
        │
Observation
        │
Digital Twin
        │
Analysis
        │
Reports
```

Typical applications include:

- Dashboards
- Operational Monitoring
- Historical Analysis
- Performance Reporting
- Engineering Reviews

The Digital Twin provides information, while humans make all operational decisions.

---

# Human-in-the-Loop Digital Twins

Many industrial Digital Twins support human decision-making.

```text
Physical System
        │
Sensors
        │
Digital Twin
        │
AI Analysis
        │
Engineer
        │
Decision
        │
Physical System
```

The Digital Twin recommends actions, but qualified engineers or operators remain responsible for approving and executing decisions.

This approach is widely used in:

- Aerospace
- Healthcare
- Manufacturing
- Energy Systems
- Critical Infrastructure

---

        │

- Industrial Automation

Closed-loop systems require rigorous validation, verification, and safety engineering.
