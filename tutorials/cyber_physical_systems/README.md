# Cyber-Physical Systems Tutorials

Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS) integrate computation, communication, sensing, control, and physical processes into intelligent engineering systems.

Unlike traditional information systems that primarily process digital information, Cyber-Physical Systems continuously interact with the physical world through sensors, actuators, embedded controllers, communication networks, and intelligent software.

These systems form the technological foundation of modern manufacturing, industrial automation, transportation, healthcare, energy systems, robotics, aerospace, defence, smart buildings, and intelligent infrastructure.

Within the FAEP Academy, Cyber-Physical Systems are presented as multidisciplinary engineering platforms that combine systems engineering, software engineering, electronics, networking, artificial intelligence, cloud computing, digital twins, and quantum technologies.

---
