# FAEP Academy Architecture

> **Enterprise Knowledge Architecture for the Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform (FAEP) Academy**

---

# Purpose

The FAEP Academy Architecture defines the organizational structure of the educational ecosystem and explains how learning resources, knowledge assets, engineering frameworks, applied research, innovation, and community engagement work together.

Rather than organizing content as isolated documents or repositories, FAEP Academy follows a layered architecture where each domain contributes to a continuous knowledge and engineering lifecycle.

This document provides the architectural view of the repository and serves as the primary reference for future expansion.

---

# Architecture Principles

The FAEP Academy architecture is guided by the following principles:

- Learning-first
- Engineering-driven
- Knowledge-centric
- Modular
- Federated
- Reusable
- Traceable
- Standards-aligned
- Extensible
- Continuously evolving

Every repository, framework, laboratory, and educational asset should align with these principles.
