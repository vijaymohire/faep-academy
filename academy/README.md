# FAEP Academy

**Learn • Build • Experiment • Innovate**

The **FAEP Academy** is an open-source engineering learning platform that brings together structured education, practical engineering, client laboratories, research, and innovation into a unified learning experience.

Rather than learning through isolated documents, learners progress through **guided learning journeys** that combine:

- Engineering tutorials
- Hands-on laboratories
- Interactive notebooks
- Engineering projects
- Client engineering laboratories
- Research activities
- Portfolio development
- Open-source collaboration

The Academy serves as the primary learning portal for the evolving **FAEP Engineering Ecosystem**.

# The FAEP Learning Experience

The Academy follows a progressive engineering learning model.

```text
Academy
      ↓
Learning Path
      ↓
Course
      ↓
Tutorial
      ↓
Notebook
      ↓
Hands-on Lab
      ↓
Client Engineering Lab
      ↓
Engineering Project
      ↓
Portfolio
      ↓
Innovation
```

This approach enables learners to combine theoretical knowledge with practical engineering experience while building a professional portfolio.

# The FAEP Ecosystem

The Academy is designed to grow alongside the FAEP ecosystem.

Current learning environments include:

| Learning Environment | Purpose |
|----------------------|---------|
| FAEP Academy | Guided learning and curriculum |
| Tutorials | Engineering concepts and theory |
| Hands-on Labs | Practical exercises |
| Client Engineering Labs | Applied engineering workflows |
| Projects | Portfolio development |
| Research | Future advanced learning |
| Innovation | Future product engineering |

As the FAEP ecosystem expands, additional repositories and engineering platforms will integrate seamlessly into the Academy without changing the learner experience.
