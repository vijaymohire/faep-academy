# Purpose

The Technology Architecture defines the enabling technology capabilities that support the educational mission of the FAEP Academy.

Rather than prescribing specific platforms, products, or implementations, it establishes a technology-neutral architectural view that enables learning, knowledge management, collaboration, personalization, research, governance, and continuous innovation.

The Technology Architecture provides a foundation that allows the Academy to evolve alongside emerging technologies while preserving architectural consistency.

This is the final major architectural viewpoint, and I think it's worth taking a slightly different approach than a traditional enterprise architecture document.

Since the Academy is technology neutral, this document should not prescribe products, vendors, cloud platforms, or implementation frameworks. Instead, it should describe the classes of technologies that enable the Academy's educational mission.

A useful distinction is:

Application Architecture → What educational services exist?
Technology Architecture → What categories of technologies can enable those services?

This keeps the document future-proof.

# Technology Capability Domains

The Academy is supported by multiple technology capability domains.

## Learning Technologies

Technologies that enable educational experiences, learning delivery, learner engagement, and educational interactions.

---

## Knowledge Technologies

Technologies that support knowledge organization, discovery, semantic relationships, and knowledge sharing.

---

## Information Technologies

Technologies that manage educational information, metadata, classifications, and educational content.

---

## Collaboration Technologies

Technologies that enable communication, teamwork, research collaboration, and institutional partnerships.

---

## Personalization Technologies

Technologies that support adaptive learning, learner profiles, recommendations, competency development, and individualized educational experiences.

---

## Analytics Technologies

Technologies that support educational insights, learning analytics, institutional reporting, and continuous improvement.

---

## Artificial Intelligence Technologies

Technologies that enhance educational assistance, intelligent tutoring, recommendations, automation, and knowledge discovery while supporting responsible and transparent use.

---

## Research Technologies

Technologies that support experimentation, simulation, innovation, scholarly collaboration, and research lifecycle activities.

---

## Governance Technologies

Technologies that support policy management, standards, quality assurance, compliance, and organizational governance.
