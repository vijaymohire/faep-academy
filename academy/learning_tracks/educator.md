# Educator Learning Track

The **Educator Learning Track** is designed for faculty members, trainers, curriculum developers, mentors, and technical educators who wish to teach modern engineering disciplines using the FAEP Academy.

Rather than focusing on a single technology, this learning track provides a structured pathway through the Academy's multidisciplinary curriculum while emphasizing teaching methodologies, practical engineering, and portfolio-based learning.

# Academic Integrity

The FAEP Academy encourages the responsible use of Artificial Intelligence as a learning assistant while maintaining academic integrity.

Educators should encourage learners to:

- Understand concepts rather than copy responses
- Cite external references where appropriate
- Explain engineering decisions in their own words
- Demonstrate practical implementation
- Document lessons learned
- Acknowledge AI-assisted work when applicable

Assessment should reward originality, engineering reasoning, experimentation, and practical application rather than simple reproduction of generated content.
