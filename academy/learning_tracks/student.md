# Student Learning Track

The **Student Learning Track** provides a structured pathway for learners beginning their engineering journey through the FAEP Academy. It combines foundational engineering concepts with hands-on learning, practical laboratories, engineering projects, and portfolio development.

Students progressively build technical knowledge, practical skills, and professional experience while exploring modern engineering disciplines including Artificial Intelligence, Quantum Computing, Systems Engineering, Digital Engineering, Robotics, and Enterprise Engineering.

# Learning Objectives

After completing this learning track, students should be able to:

- Understand engineering fundamentals.
- Complete structured Academy learning pathways.
- Apply engineering concepts through practical laboratories.
- Build engineering projects and technical documentation.
- Develop an engineering portfolio.
- Prepare for advanced study and professional careers.

# Student Success Tips

Successful learners should:

- Study consistently.
- Practice through hands-on laboratories.
- Build projects instead of only reading theory.
- Ask questions and collaborate with others.
- Document engineering work.
- Maintain an up-to-date GitHub portfolio.
- Continue learning across multiple engineering disciplines.
