# Researcher Learning Track

The **Researcher Learning Track** prepares learners to conduct multidisciplinary engineering research using the FAEP Academy. It combines scientific methods, systems engineering, Artificial Intelligence, Quantum Computing, Digital Engineering, and innovation practices to support research, experimentation, validation, and technology development.

Learners are encouraged to build reproducible research, publish engineering artifacts, contribute to open-source projects, and develop innovative solutions to real-world challenges.

# Learning Objectives

After completing this learning track, learners should be able to:

- Apply scientific and engineering research methodologies.
- Design and execute reproducible experiments.
- Evaluate emerging technologies and engineering approaches.
- Develop prototypes, proofs of concept, and research artifacts.
- Publish engineering documentation and technical reports.
- Contribute to innovation and open-source research.

# QAI Applied Research

The FAEP Academy enables researchers to move beyond theoretical learning by applying research ideas within the **QAI Lab** and the broader QAI engineering ecosystem.

Researchers are encouraged to transform research questions into practical engineering investigations through structured experimentation, validation, and prototype development.

The objective is to bridge the gap between:

- Fundamental Research
- Applied Research
- Engineering Validation
- Product Engineering
- Technology Transfer
