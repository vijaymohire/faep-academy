# Enterprise Architect Learning Track

The **Enterprise Architect Learning Track** prepares professionals to design, integrate, modernize, and govern enterprise technology ecosystems using multidisciplinary engineering principles from the FAEP Academy.

Learners develop the knowledge and practical skills needed to align business strategy, enterprise architecture, digital engineering, Artificial Intelligence, cloud platforms, cybersecurity, and emerging technologies.

# Learning Objectives

After completing this learning track, learners should be able to:

- Design enterprise architectures aligned with business objectives.
- Apply systems thinking to enterprise transformation.
- Integrate AI, cloud, cybersecurity, and digital engineering into enterprise solutions.
- Evaluate enterprise technology portfolios.
- Govern enterprise architecture using engineering best practices.
- Lead digital transformation initiatives.
