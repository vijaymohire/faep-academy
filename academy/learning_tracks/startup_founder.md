# Startup Founder Learning Track

The **Startup Founder Learning Track** prepares entrepreneurs to transform engineering ideas into innovative products, platforms, and technology ventures using the multidisciplinary capabilities of the FAEP Academy.

Learners progress from technical foundations through research, product engineering, validation, intellectual property, and commercialization while leveraging the broader QAI engineering ecosystem.

# Learning Objectives

After completing this learning track, learners should be able to:

- Identify technology opportunities.
- Validate engineering ideas.
- Build Proofs of Concept (PoCs).
- Develop minimum viable products (MVPs).
- Apply systems engineering principles.
- Build technology portfolios.
- Understand intellectual property and commercialization.
- Scale engineering innovations into startup ventures.
