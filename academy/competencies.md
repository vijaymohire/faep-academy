# Competencies

The FAEP Academy is designed around **competency-based engineering education**.

Rather than simply completing courses, learners progressively develop practical engineering competencies through tutorials, laboratories, notebooks, engineering projects, applied engineering, and portfolio development.

Competencies represent the knowledge, practical skills, engineering capability, and professional experience gained throughout the Academy.

```text
      ↓

      ↓

      ↓

      ↓

      ↓

      ↓

      ↓

```
