# Learning Journeys

While **Learning Paths** describe the recommended sequence of engineering topics, **Learning Journeys** organize those paths around real learner personas and career aspirations.

Every journey combines:

- Engineering Courses
- Tutorials
- Hands-on Labs
- Jupyter Notebooks
- Engineering Projects
- FAEP Client Lab
- Engineering Portfolio
- Research & Innovation

The journeys are flexible and can be adapted to suit individual goals.
