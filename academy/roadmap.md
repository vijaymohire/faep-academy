# FAEP Academy Roadmap

The FAEP Academy is an evolving engineering education platform designed to support continuous learning, practical engineering, research, and innovation.

This roadmap outlines the planned evolution of the Academy and its integration with the wider FAEP Engineering Ecosystem.

# Academy Vision

The long-term vision is to create an open, engineering-focused learning ecosystem where learners can:

- Learn
- Practice
- Experiment
- Build
- Apply
- Research
- Innovate
- Contribute

The Academy combines structured education with practical engineering experiences and open-source collaboration.
