# Getting Started

Welcome to the **FAEP Academy**.

The Academy is designed to help you progress from foundational concepts to practical engineering through tutorials, notebooks, hands-on laboratories, engineering projects, and applied engineering experiences.

Whether your interests include Artificial Intelligence, Quantum Computing, Enterprise Engineering, Intelligent Systems, Robotics, Space Systems, Quantum AI, or Federated Autonomous Ecosystems, the Academy provides a structured yet flexible learning experience.
