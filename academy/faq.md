# General Questions

## What is the FAEP Academy?

The FAEP Academy is an open-source engineering learning platform focused on Artificial Intelligence, Quantum Computing, Enterprise Engineering, Intelligent Systems, Robotics, Space Systems, Digital Twins, and the Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform (FAEP).

---

## Who is the Academy designed for?

The Academy supports:

- Students
- Working Professionals
- Researchers
- Educators
- Architects
- Startup Founders
- Technology Consultants
- Lifelong Learners

---

## Is the Academy free?

Yes.

The learning resources provided through the Academy are designed to support open and self-paced learning.

---

## Do I need prior experience?

No.

Beginners should start with:

- [Getting Started](getting_started.md)
- [Foundations](courses/foundations/README.md)

More advanced learners can begin with the courses that match their experience.

# Learning Questions

## How should I study?

The recommended approach is:

```text
Course
      ↓
Tutorial
      ↓
Notebook
      ↓
Academy Lab
      ↓
Engineering Project
      ↓
FAEP Client Lab
      ↓
Portfolio
```

---

## Can I learn at my own pace?

Yes.

The Academy is designed for self-paced learning. There are no fixed schedules or mandatory deadlines.

---

## Do I have to complete every course?

No.

Learning Paths provide recommendations, but you can choose courses based on your goals and interests.

---

## Can I combine multiple learning paths?

Yes.

Many learners combine AI, Quantum Computing, Enterprise Engineering, Robotics, or Research learning paths.

---

# Practical Engineering

## What are Academy Labs?

Academy Labs are guided exercises that reinforce concepts introduced in tutorials and notebooks.

Explore:

- [Hands-on Labs](../labs/)

---

## What is the FAEP Client Lab?

The **FAEP Client Lab** is a companion engineering environment that extends the Academy into applied engineering.

It includes:

- Engineering SDKs
- Datasets
- Providers
- Dashboards
- Templates
- Execution environments
- Client engineering scenarios

**External Learning Resource**

**FAEP Client Lab**

https://github.com/vijaymohire/faep-client-lab

---

## When should I use the FAEP Client Lab?

After completing tutorials, notebooks, Academy Labs, and engineering projects.

The Client Lab is intended to help learners apply their knowledge to realistic engineering scenarios.

# Portfolio Development

## Should I maintain my own GitHub repository?

Yes.

Maintaining your own repositories helps you:

- Track progress
- Build a professional portfolio
- Demonstrate practical engineering skills
- Share your work with others

---

## Can I contribute to the Academy?

Yes.

Contributions may include:
