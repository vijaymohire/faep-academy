# 1. Purpose

This document defines the certification taxonomy used throughout the FAEP Academy.

Certifications formally recognize the successful demonstration of competencies through structured learning, practical experience, assessments, and professional validation.

They provide learners with measurable milestones that support career development and lifelong learning.

---

# 2. Design Principles

Certifications should be:

- Competency-based
- Assessment-driven
- Industry aligned
- Portable
- Stackable
- Verifiable
- Knowledge Graph ready

# 4. Certification Categories

## Foundation Certifications

Purpose

Validate foundational knowledge.

Examples

- Programming Foundations
- AI Foundations
- Quantum Foundations
- Systems Engineering Foundations

---

## Practitioner Certifications

Purpose

Validate practical implementation skills.

Examples

- AI Practitioner
- Quantum Computing Practitioner
- Cloud Practitioner
- Software Engineering Practitioner

---

## Professional Certifications

Purpose

Validate production-ready capabilities.

Examples

- Professional AI Engineer
- Professional Systems Engineer
- Professional Enterprise Architect
- Professional Cloud Engineer

---

## Specialist Certifications

Purpose

Recognize expertise within specialized domains.

Examples

- Quantum Algorithms Specialist
- AI Security Specialist
- Robotics Specialist
- Digital Twin Specialist

---

## Research Certifications

Purpose

Recognize research capability and innovation.

Examples

- Quantum Research
- AI Research
- Applied Research
- Scientific Computing

---

## Leadership Certifications

Purpose

Recognize organizational and technical leadership.

Examples

- Innovation Leadership
- Technical Leadership
- Architecture Leadership
- Digital Transformation Leadership

FAEP Architecture Recommendation

One enhancement that would make the FAEP Academy stand out is to organize certifications into three complementary layers rather than treating them as standalone credentials:

Academic Certifications
│
├── Foundation
├── Intermediate
├── Advanced
└── Research
│
├───────────────────────────────┐
│                               │
Professional Certifications     │
│                               │
├── AI Engineer                 │
├── Quantum Engineer            │
├── Systems Engineer            │
├── Cloud Engineer              │
└── Enterprise Architect        │
│                               │
├───────────────────────────────┘
│
Micro-Credentials
│
├── Individual Skills
├── Individual Competencies
├── Technology Badges
├── Lab Completion
└── Project Achievement

This layered approach allows learners to accumulate micro-credentials (for specific skills and projects), combine them into professional certifications (demonstrating role-based competence), and ultimately earn broader academic certifications that represent comprehensive mastery of a knowledge area. It also provides a scalable framework for future integration with digital badges, competency passports, and AI-driven learning recommendations.
