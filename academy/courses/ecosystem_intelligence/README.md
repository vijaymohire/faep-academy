Ecosystem Intelligence represents one of the emerging engineering disciplines within the FAEP ecosystem, enabling collaborative intelligence across distributed environments.

# Course Overview

Ecosystem Intelligence extends traditional systems engineering by focusing on collaboration between multiple intelligent systems operating across organizational and technological boundaries.

The course explores engineering principles for building connected ecosystems that integrate Artificial Intelligence, Digital Twins, Cyber-Physical Systems, Knowledge Graphs, cloud platforms, autonomous agents, and enterprise systems.

---

# Learning Objectives

After completing this course, learners should be able to:

- Understand Ecosystem Intelligence concepts.
- Explain federated and distributed intelligent systems.
- Understand ecosystem architectures and interoperability.
- Apply systems thinking across organizational boundaries.
- Explore autonomous collaboration between intelligent systems.
- Complete tutorials, laboratories, notebooks, and engineering projects.
- Build a professional Ecosystem Intelligence portfolio.
