# Foundations

Build the knowledge, engineering mindset, and practical skills that form the foundation of the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

The Foundations course serves as the entry point to the FAEP Academy and prepares learners for advanced engineering topics through structured tutorials, hands-on labs, interactive notebooks, engineering projects, and portfolio development.

# Course Overview

The Foundations course introduces the engineering principles, technologies, and methodologies that underpin the FAEP ecosystem.

Rather than teaching individual technologies in isolation, the course provides a structured pathway that prepares learners for advanced topics including Artificial Intelligence, Quantum Computing, Systems Engineering, Digital Engineering, Innovation, and Applied Research.

---

# Learning Objectives

After completing this course, learners should be able to:

- Understand the FAEP ecosystem and engineering philosophy.
- Apply systems thinking and engineering principles.
- Navigate the FAEP repositories and learning resources.
- Complete tutorials, labs, notebooks, and engineering projects.
- Build a professional engineering portfolio.
- Prepare for advanced FAEP learning tracks.
