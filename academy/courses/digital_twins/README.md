# Digital Twins

Develop the knowledge and engineering skills required to design, implement, and operate Digital Twins that integrate physical systems, digital models, real-time data, simulation, analytics, and intelligent decision support.

Digital Twins play a key role in modern engineering by connecting the physical and digital worlds across manufacturing, healthcare, aerospace, smart cities, energy systems, transportation, and industrial automation.

# Course Overview

Digital Twins provide virtual representations of physical systems that continuously synchronize with operational data to support monitoring, simulation, optimization, prediction, and decision making.

This course introduces Digital Twins from an engineering perspective and prepares learners to develop intelligent digital representations using systems engineering, AI, IoT, Cyber-Physical Systems, simulation, and lifecycle management.

---

# Learning Objectives

After completing this course, learners should be able to:

- Understand Digital Twin concepts and architectures.
- Explain the relationship between physical and digital systems.
- Understand data integration and synchronization.
- Apply systems engineering principles to Digital Twins.
- Explore AI-enabled Digital Twins.
- Complete tutorials, laboratories, notebooks, and engineering projects.
- Build a Digital Twins engineering portfolio.
