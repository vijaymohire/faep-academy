# Cyber-Physical Systems

Develop the knowledge and engineering skills required to design, integrate, and secure Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS) that combine computing, networking, sensing, control, and physical processes.

This course introduces Cyber-Physical Systems from an engineering perspective and prepares learners to build intelligent, resilient, and trustworthy systems across domains including manufacturing, robotics, transportation, healthcare, aerospace, and smart infrastructure.

# Course Overview

Cyber-Physical Systems integrate software, embedded computing, communication networks, sensors, actuators, and physical processes into intelligent engineering systems.

This course provides a structured pathway through tutorials, laboratories, notebooks, engineering projects, and the FAEP Client Lab, preparing learners to develop modern intelligent systems that are safe, reliable, secure, and resilient.

---

# Learning Objectives

After completing this course, learners should be able to:

- Understand the principles of Cyber-Physical Systems.
- Explain the interaction between cyber and physical components.
- Understand sensing, control, networking, and embedded computing.
- Apply cybersecurity principles to Cyber-Physical Systems.
- Explore Digital Twins and intelligent automation.
- Complete practical tutorials, labs, notebooks, and engineering projects.
- Build a professional engineering portfolio.
