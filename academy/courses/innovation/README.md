# Course Overview

Innovation Engineering applies structured methods to discover opportunities, solve complex problems, develop new technologies, and transform research into practical products and services.

This course introduces innovation from an engineering perspective, integrating systems engineering, applied research, technology strategy, product development, intellectual property, and entrepreneurship.

Learners progress through tutorials, laboratories, notebooks, engineering projects, and the FAEP Client Lab to gain practical innovation engineering experience.

---

# Learning Objectives

After completing this course, learners should be able to:

- Understand innovation engineering principles.
- Apply structured innovation methodologies.
- Explore applied research and technology transfer.
- Understand product and technology lifecycle management.
- Develop engineering innovation projects.
- Understand intellectual property fundamentals.
- Build a professional innovation portfolio.
