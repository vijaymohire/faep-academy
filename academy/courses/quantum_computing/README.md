# Course Overview

Quantum Computing applies the principles of quantum mechanics to information processing, enabling computational approaches that are fundamentally different from classical computing.

This course introduces quantum information, qubits, quantum gates, quantum circuits, algorithms, error correction, quantum programming, and practical engineering considerations for developing quantum-enabled solutions.

Learners progress through tutorials, laboratories, notebooks, engineering projects, and the FAEP Client Lab to gain practical Quantum Computing experience.

---

# Learning Objectives

After completing this course, learners should be able to:

- Understand quantum information and quantum computation.
- Explain qubits, superposition, entanglement, and measurement.
- Design and analyze quantum circuits.
- Explore quantum algorithms and quantum programming.
- Understand current quantum hardware platforms.
- Develop quantum engineering projects.
- Build a professional Quantum Computing portfolio.
