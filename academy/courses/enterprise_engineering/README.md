# Enterprise Engineering

Develop the knowledge and engineering skills required to design, integrate, operate, and continuously improve modern enterprises through systems thinking, enterprise architecture, digital transformation, governance, and intelligent engineering.

Enterprise Engineering combines business strategy, engineering principles, information technology, organizational design, and innovation to create resilient, adaptive, and high-performing enterprises.

# Course Overview

Enterprise Engineering applies engineering methodologies to the design, transformation, governance, and continuous improvement of complex enterprises.

This course introduces enterprise engineering from a multidisciplinary perspective, integrating systems engineering, enterprise architecture, software engineering, artificial intelligence, digital engineering, and organizational transformation.

Learners progress through tutorials, laboratories, notebooks, engineering projects, and the FAEP Client Lab to gain practical enterprise engineering experience.

---

# Learning Objectives

After completing this course, learners should be able to:

- Understand Enterprise Engineering principles.
- Apply systems thinking to enterprise transformation.
- Understand enterprise architecture and governance.
- Explore digital transformation strategies.
- Integrate AI, Digital Twins, and intelligent systems within enterprises.
- Complete enterprise engineering projects.
- Build a professional enterprise engineering portfolio.
