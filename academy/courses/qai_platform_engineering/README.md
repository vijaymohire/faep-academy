# QAI Platform Engineering

Develop the knowledge and engineering skills required to design, build, integrate, deploy, and operate Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI) platforms that combine classical computing, Artificial Intelligence, quantum computing, cloud infrastructure, enterprise systems, orchestration, and intelligent automation.

QAI Platform Engineering represents the convergence of modern computing technologies into an integrated engineering platform for next-generation intelligent systems.

# Course Overview

QAI Platform Engineering applies systems engineering principles to the design and operation of integrated Quantum AI platforms.

Rather than focusing on individual technologies, the course explores how Artificial Intelligence, Quantum Computing, Hybrid Quantum Systems, cloud infrastructure, orchestration platforms, middleware, Digital Twins, Cyber-Physical Systems, and enterprise engineering work together as a cohesive engineering platform.

Learners progress through tutorials, laboratories, notebooks, engineering projects, and the FAEP Client Lab to gain practical QAI Platform Engineering experience.

---

# Learning Objectives

After completing this course, learners should be able to:

- Understand QAI Platform Engineering principles.
- Design hybrid AI and Quantum Computing architectures.
- Understand platform engineering and orchestration.
- Integrate cloud, AI, quantum, and enterprise technologies.
- Develop scalable Quantum AI engineering solutions.
- Complete QAI platform engineering projects.
- Build a professional Quantum AI engineering portfolio.
