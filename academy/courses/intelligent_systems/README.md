# Course Overview

Intelligent Systems integrate multiple engineering disciplines to create systems capable of perception, reasoning, learning, decision making, adaptation, and autonomous operation.

This course introduces Intelligent Systems from an engineering perspective, integrating Artificial Intelligence, Cyber-Physical Systems, Digital Twins, Robotics, IoT, Knowledge Graphs, and Enterprise Engineering.

Learners progress through tutorials, laboratories, notebooks, engineering projects, and the FAEP Client Lab to gain practical Intelligent Systems engineering experience.

---

# Learning Objectives

After completing this course, learners should be able to:

- Understand Intelligent System architectures.
- Explain perception, reasoning, planning, and decision-making.
- Integrate AI, Digital Twins, IoT, and Cyber-Physical Systems.
- Design adaptive and autonomous engineering systems.
- Develop Intelligent Systems engineering projects.
- Build a professional engineering portfolio.
