# Course Overview

Research Engineering provides a structured engineering approach for discovering, evaluating, developing, validating, and transitioning new technologies into practical solutions.

This course introduces research methodologies, experimental design, systems engineering, technology readiness, validation, intellectual property awareness, and engineering documentation.

Learners progress through tutorials, laboratories, notebooks, engineering projects, and the FAEP Client Lab while developing research-oriented engineering capabilities.

---

# Learning Objectives

After completing this course, learners should be able to:

- Understand engineering research methodologies.
- Design and conduct structured experiments.
- Develop research prototypes.
- Apply engineering validation techniques.
- Understand technology readiness concepts.
- Document research outcomes effectively.
- Build a professional research portfolio.
