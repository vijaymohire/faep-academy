# Mission Systems

Develop the engineering knowledge and practical skills required to design, integrate, validate, operate, and sustain complex Mission Systems that combine software, hardware, communications, sensors, decision support, autonomy, Artificial Intelligence, and systems engineering.

Mission Systems provide the operational capabilities required for aerospace, defense, transportation, healthcare, industrial automation, smart infrastructure, robotics, and space missions.

# Course Overview

Mission Systems Engineering focuses on the integration of multiple engineering disciplines into reliable, resilient, safe, and mission-oriented operational systems.

This course introduces Mission Systems from an engineering perspective, integrating Systems Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, Cyber-Physical Systems, Digital Twins, communications, sensing, autonomy, and enterprise operations.

Learners progress through tutorials, laboratories, notebooks, engineering projects, and the FAEP Client Lab to gain practical Mission Systems engineering experience.

---

# Learning Objectives

After completing this course, learners should be able to:

- Understand Mission Systems engineering principles.
- Design mission-oriented architectures.
- Integrate software, hardware, communication, and sensing systems.
- Apply Systems Engineering and Mission Assurance concepts.
- Understand reliability, resilience, and operational readiness.
- Develop Mission Systems engineering projects.
- Build a professional engineering portfolio.
