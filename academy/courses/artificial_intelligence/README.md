Develop a strong engineering foundation in Artificial Intelligence through structured tutorials, practical laboratories, interactive notebooks, engineering projects, and applied learning within the FAEP ecosystem.

# Course Overview

Artificial Intelligence is one of the core engineering disciplines within the FAEP Academy.

Rather than focusing on individual software frameworks or programming libraries, this course emphasizes engineering principles, system design, practical implementation, and responsible deployment of intelligent systems.

Learners progress through tutorials, laboratories, notebooks, engineering projects, and the FAEP Client Lab to develop practical AI engineering skills.

---

# Learning Objectives

After completing this course, learners should be able to:

- Understand the fundamental concepts of Artificial Intelligence.
- Distinguish between Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Generative AI, and Quantum AI.
- Apply AI engineering principles to real-world problems.
- Complete AI tutorials, laboratories, and notebooks.
- Develop AI engineering projects.
- Build an AI engineering portfolio.
- Prepare for advanced AI and Quantum AI learning tracks.
