# Space Systems

Develop the knowledge and engineering skills required to design, integrate, deploy, operate, and sustain modern Space Systems that combine spacecraft, satellites, communications, robotics, Artificial Intelligence, autonomy, mission engineering, and ground systems.

Space Systems Engineering integrates multiple engineering disciplines to support scientific exploration, Earth observation, communications, navigation, defense, and commercial space missions.

# Course Overview

Space Systems Engineering applies systems engineering principles to the complete lifecycle of space missions, including spacecraft design, payload integration, communications, navigation, mission operations, and long-term sustainability.

This course provides a structured pathway through tutorials, laboratories, notebooks, engineering projects, and the FAEP Client Lab to develop practical space systems engineering capabilities.

---

# Learning Objectives

After completing this course, learners should be able to:

- Understand Space Systems engineering principles.
- Explain spacecraft and mission architectures.
- Understand satellite subsystems and payload integration.
- Explore autonomous and intelligent space systems.
- Apply systems engineering to mission design and operations.
- Develop Space Systems engineering projects.
- Build a professional engineering portfolio.
