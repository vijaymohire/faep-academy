# Purpose

The **Competency Personalization** architecture enables the FAEP Academy ecosystem to tailor educational experiences based on an individual's competencies, capability gaps, professional objectives, and continuous development needs.

Rather than presenting identical learning pathways to every learner, competency personalization aligns educational content, assessments, projects, certifications, and recommendations with each learner's current capabilities and desired competency outcomes.

This architecture supports competency-based education, workforce development, lifelong learning, and professional growth across academic and enterprise environments.

This document transitions personalization from general learning to professional capability development, aligning well with the competency framework and competency control plane you've already established.

# Governance

Competency personalization shall:

- Use validated competency frameworks
- Maintain transparent competency assessments
- Preserve learner ownership of achievements
- Support educator and mentor oversight
- Protect learner information
- Enable competency traceability
- Support continuous competency evaluation
- Align with organizational and professional standards
