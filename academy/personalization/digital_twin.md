# Purpose

The **Digital Twin** architecture defines a continuously evolving digital representation of a learner within the FAEP Academy ecosystem.

A Digital Learner Twin integrates learner profiles, competencies, educational progress, contextual information, learning history, achievements, analytics, and AI insights into a unified representation that supports personalized education, professional development, and lifelong learning.

Rather than acting as a static learner record, the Digital Twin continuously reflects the learner's educational journey and provides a foundation for intelligent educational guidance and future autonomous learning ecosystems.

This is arguably the most forward-looking document in the entire Personalization architecture and aligns perfectly with the long-term FAEP vision.

It should not be a simulation document. Instead, it should define the Digital Learner Twin as a living enterprise representation of a learner that continuously evolves throughout lifelong learning.

The architecture progression now becomes:

Learner Profiles
        │
        ▼
Context Management
        │
        ▼
Analytics + AI
        │
        ▼
Digital Twin
        │
        ▼
Autonomous Personalization
