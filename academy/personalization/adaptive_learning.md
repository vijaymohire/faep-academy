# Purpose

The **Adaptive Learning** architecture enables the FAEP Academy ecosystem to dynamically personalize educational experiences based on learner profiles, competencies, learning progress, objectives, context, and analytics.

Rather than delivering a fixed curriculum to every learner, adaptive learning continuously adjusts educational pathways, instructional strategies, assessments, recommendations, and learning resources to maximize learning effectiveness and engagement.

This naturally follows Learner Profiles.

The relationship is:

Learner Profiles → Who is the learner?
Adaptive Learning → How should the learning experience adapt?

This document should focus on the mechanisms that dynamically tailor educational experiences based on learner characteristics, progress, competencies, and context.
