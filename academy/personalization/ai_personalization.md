# Purpose

The **AI Personalization** architecture defines how Artificial Intelligence capabilities enhance personalized education throughout the FAEP Academy ecosystem.

By combining learner profiles, contextual information, competencies, educational objectives, analytics, and semantic knowledge, AI assists learners, instructors, researchers, and organizations by delivering intelligent, adaptive, and explainable educational experiences.

AI complements human educators and institutional policies by providing timely insights, recommendations, automation, and decision support while maintaining transparency, learner autonomy, and educator oversight.

This is one of the most important documents in the Personalization architecture because it elevates personalization from a rule-based capability to an intelligent, adaptive, and continuously learning enterprise capability.

The progression now becomes:

Unlike the earlier AI Control Plane, which governs AI services across the Academy, AI Personalization focuses specifically on applying AI to create individualized educational experiences.

At this point, the Personalization architecture has evolved into a complete enterprise capability:

Learner Profiles define who the learner is.
Context Management captures the current situation.
Adaptive Learning adjusts how learning is delivered.
Recommendation Engine determines what should be suggested.
Personalization Rules ensure consistent and governed decisions.
Competency Personalization aligns learning with capability development.
Career Personalization aligns learning with professional objectives.
AI Personalization provides the intelligent reasoning layer that continuously orchestrates and improves all personalization services across the FAEP Academy ecosystem.
