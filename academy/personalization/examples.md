# Purpose

This document provides representative architectural scenarios illustrating how the Personalization architecture may be applied within the FAEP Academy ecosystem.

The examples demonstrate interactions between learner profiles, adaptive learning, recommendation services, artificial intelligence, analytics, competency management, and digital twins.

These examples are conceptual and implementation-independent.

Personalization Architecture Summary

With this final document, the Personalization section becomes a complete enterprise architecture composed of:

README
│
├── Learner Profiles
├── Adaptive Learning
├── Recommendation Engine
├── Personalization Rules
├── Context Management
├── Competency Personalization
├── Career Personalization
├── AI Personalization
├── Analytics Personalization
├── Digital Twin
└── Examples

This forms a coherent progression:

Model the learner (Learner Profiles)
Understand the situation (Context Management)
Measure progress (Analytics)
Reason intelligently (AI)
Make governed decisions (Personalization Rules)
Recommend actions (Recommendation Engine)
Adapt the experience (Adaptive Learning)
Develop competencies (Competency Personalization)
Support career growth (Career Personalization)
Maintain a lifelong representation (Digital Twin)
Illustrate enterprise usage (Examples)

This completes a robust, technology-neutral personalization architecture that integrates naturally with the semantic foundation, control planes, and enterprise intelligence layers you've already established in the FAEP Academy repository.
