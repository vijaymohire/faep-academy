# Purpose

The **Career Personalization** architecture enables the FAEP Academy ecosystem to align educational experiences, competency development, certifications, and professional learning with each learner's career aspirations and workforce objectives.

Rather than providing generic career guidance, the architecture continuously adapts recommendations based on competencies, learning progress, professional interests, organizational requirements, and evolving industry needs.

Career personalization supports learners throughout their professional journey, from education and certification to career advancement and lifelong learning.

This is the natural extension of Competency Personalization.

The progression becomes:

Learner Profiles
        │
        ▼
Adaptive Learning
        │
        ▼
Recommendation Engine
        │
        ▼
Personalization Rules
        │
        ▼
Context Management
        │
        ▼
Competency Personalization
        │
        ▼
Career Personalization

While Competency Personalization focuses on developing capabilities, Career Personalization focuses on applying those capabilities toward career goals and lifelong professional growth.
