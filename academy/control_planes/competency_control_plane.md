# Purpose

The **Competency Control Plane** orchestrates the identification, development, validation, and continuous improvement of competencies across the FAEP Academy ecosystem.

It connects learning outcomes with professional skills, competency frameworks, certifications, roles, and career pathways to ensure learners develop capabilities aligned with academic and industry requirements.

Rather than assessing individual learning activities, the Competency Control Plane manages **the long-term development and validation of professional competencies**.


This is the natural successor to the Assessment Control Plane.

The distinction should be:

Assessment Control Plane → Measures learning outcomes.
Competency Control Plane → Manages professional capabilities and lifelong competency development.

It becomes the bridge between education and industry.

Architecture Evolution

With this document completed, you've established the Academy's core educational value chain:

Educational Content
        │
        ▼
Learning Experience
        │
        ▼
Assessment
        │
        ▼
Competency Development
        │
        ▼
Portfolio
        │
        ▼
Certification
        │
        ▼
Career & Professional Growth

This is a strong enterprise education model because each control plane owns a distinct business capability:

Content → manages educational assets.
Learning → orchestrates learning journeys.
Assessment → validates learning outcomes.
Competency → manages professional capabilities.

The next logical document, Portfolio Control Plane, will extend this chain by managing how validated competencies, projects, research, and achievements are organized into a learner's professional portfolio, paving the way for the later Certification Control Plane.
