# Purpose

The **Research Control Plane** orchestrates research, innovation, experimentation, knowledge creation, and technology transfer across the FAEP Academy ecosystem.

It coordinates research projects, publications, innovation initiatives, laboratories, collaborative studies, and emerging technologies while ensuring that new knowledge continuously enriches educational content and professional practice.

Rather than delivering educational content, the Research Control Plane manages **the lifecycle of research and innovation**.

This control plane is particularly important for the FAEP Academy because research is not just an academic activity—it is a continuous innovation pipeline that feeds new knowledge back into the educational ecosystem.

Unlike the other control planes, the Research Control Plane should emphasize knowledge creation, innovation management, experimentation, and technology transfer, making it the bridge between research and education.
