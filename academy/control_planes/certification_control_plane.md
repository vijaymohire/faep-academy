# Purpose

The **Certification Control Plane** orchestrates the issuance, validation, management, and lifecycle of certifications, credentials, badges, and academic achievements across the FAEP Academy ecosystem.

It transforms validated competencies into trusted credentials that recognize learner achievements and support professional development, workforce readiness, and lifelong learning.

Rather than measuring competencies, the Certification Control Plane manages **the formal recognition and verification of learner accomplishments**.

This control plane completes the transition from learning to recognized achievement.

The progression is now:

Learning → Assessment → Competency → Certification

Unlike the Competency Control Plane, which manages capabilities, the Certification Control Plane manages the formal recognition, verification, issuance, and lifecycle of credentials.

The Certification Control Plane is responsible for:

- Certification lifecycle management
- Digital credential management
- Badge management
- Credential verification
- Certification eligibility validation
- Certification issuance
- Certification renewal

- Academic transcript coordination
- Achievement recognition
- Certification governance
