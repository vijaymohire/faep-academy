# Purpose

The **Governance Control Plane** orchestrates policies, standards, decision-making, compliance, quality assurance, risk management, and institutional oversight across the FAEP Academy ecosystem.

It establishes the governance framework that ensures educational excellence, operational consistency, responsible innovation, regulatory compliance, and continuous improvement while supporting transparency and accountability.

Rather than executing operational activities, the Governance Control Plane defines **how the Academy is directed, controlled, monitored, and continuously improved**.

This is arguably the highest-level enterprise control plane because it establishes the policies, standards, compliance, quality, and oversight that apply to every other control plane.

Unlike the Platform Control Plane (which manages technical operations), the Governance Control Plane manages organizational decision-making, accountability, risk, and compliance across the FAEP Academy ecosystem.

Architecture Evolution

With the Governance Control Plane complete, the Academy now has a clear enterprise oversight layer:

                 Enterprise Governance Layer

                Governance Control Plane
                         │
        ┌────────────────┼────────────────┐
        ▼                ▼                ▼
    Policies         Standards        Compliance
        │                │                │
        └────────────────┼────────────────┘
                         ▼
      Learning • Research • AI • Analytics
      Platform • Security • Operations
      Automation • Certification • Content
                         │
                         ▼
           Trusted FAEP Academy Ecosystem

At this point, your control plane architecture naturally organizes into four tiers:

Business Domain Control Planes – Learning, Content, Knowledge, Assessment, Competency, Learner, Instructor, Certification, Research.
Intelligence Control Planes – AI and Analytics.
Governance & Trust Control Planes – Governance (and Security, once completed).
Platform & Operations Control Planes – Platform, Operations, Integration, and Automation.

This layered architecture aligns closely with enterprise architecture best practices by separating domain capabilities from cross-cutting intelligence, governance, and operational concerns while keeping clear ownership boundaries.
