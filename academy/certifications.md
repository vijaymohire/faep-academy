# Certifications

The **FAEP Academy Certification Framework** recognizes engineering knowledge, practical skills, and demonstrated competencies developed through structured learning, engineering projects, and applied engineering experiences.

Unlike traditional examination-based certification programs, the Academy emphasizes **learning by building**.

Certification is intended to complement—not replace—practical engineering experience and portfolio development.

# Certification Philosophy

The Academy encourages learners to demonstrate competency through practical engineering.

```text
Learn
      ↓
Practice
      ↓
Experiment
      ↓
Build
      ↓
Apply
      ↓
Validate
      ↓
Portfolio
      ↓
Certification
```

Certification represents demonstrated engineering capability rather than simply completing a course.

---

# Certification Framework

The proposed certification framework is organized into progressive levels.

| Level | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Foundation | Engineering fundamentals and core concepts |
| Practitioner | Practical implementation and hands-on engineering |
| Professional | Multi-domain engineering competency |
| Advanced | Complex engineering systems and architecture |
| Expert | Research, innovation, leadership, and ecosystem contribution |

# Competency-Based Certification

Certification may be based on evidence gathered through:

- Course completion
- Tutorials
- Hands-on Labs
- Jupyter Notebooks
- Engineering Projects
- Portfolio reviews
- Open-source contributions
- Applied engineering activities

The Academy encourages every learner to build a professional engineering portfolio.

- Software projects
- Architecture diagrams
- Technical documentation
- Research reports
