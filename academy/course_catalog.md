# Course Catalog

The **FAEP Academy Course Catalog** provides a structured overview of all engineering courses available across the Academy.

Every course is designed around the philosophy of **Learn → Practice → Build → Apply → Innovate**, combining structured learning with practical engineering experiences.

# Engineering Learning Workflow

Every course follows the same engineering progression.

```text
Course
      ↓
Tutorial
      ↓
Notebook
      ↓
Academy Hands-on Lab
      ↓
Engineering Project
      ↓
FAEP Client Lab
      ↓
Engineering Portfolio
      ↓
Research
      ↓
Innovation
```

# Beyond the Academy

The FAEP Academy extends beyond traditional engineering education.

Learners can continue into:

- Engineering Projects
- FAEP Client Lab
- Research Activities
- Innovation Programs
- Open-Source Contributions
- Professional Engineering Portfolio Development

The Academy provides the educational foundation, while the **FAEP Client Lab** enables learners to apply those skills in practical engineering environments.
