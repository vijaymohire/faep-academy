# Learning Paths

The FAEP Academy provides structured learning paths that help learners progress from fundamental engineering concepts to practical engineering implementation.

Every learning path combines:

- Engineering Courses
- Tutorials
- Hands-on Labs
- Jupyter Notebooks
- Engineering Projects
- FAEP Client Lab
- Portfolio Development
- Research & Innovation

Although recommended paths are provided, learners are encouraged to customize their own journey based on interests and career goals.
