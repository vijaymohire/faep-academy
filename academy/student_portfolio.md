# Student Portfolio

The **FAEP Academy Student Portfolio** is a personal record of your engineering journey.

Rather than simply completing courses, learners are encouraged to demonstrate their knowledge through practical engineering artifacts, projects, technical documentation, research, and open-source contributions.

Your portfolio becomes evidence of your engineering capability and professional growth.
