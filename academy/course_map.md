# Course Map

The **FAEP Academy Course Map** illustrates how engineering knowledge is organized across the Academy.

Unlike traditional education where subjects are studied independently, the FAEP Academy connects engineering disciplines into a unified ecosystem that progresses from foundational knowledge to practical engineering, research, and innovation.

# Applied Engineering

After completing Academy projects, learners can continue into the **FAEP Client Lab**, which extends learning into practical engineering environments.

The Client Lab includes:

- Engineering SDKs
- Engineering Providers
- Datasets
- Dashboards
- Templates
- Execution Environments
- Applied Engineering Workflows
- Client Engineering Scenarios
