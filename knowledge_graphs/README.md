# Knowledge Graphs

> **FAEP Knowledge Assets | Enterprise Semantic Intelligence | Quantum AI Foundation**

---

# Overview

The **Knowledge Graphs** repository provides the semantic knowledge foundation for the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

As enterprises evolve from traditional information systems to **Artificial Intelligence (AI)** and **Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI)** driven organizations, structured knowledge becomes a strategic enterprise asset. Knowledge Graphs capture concepts, relationships, dependencies, ontologies, engineering knowledge, enterprise architectures, products, research, and modernization pathways in a reusable and machine-understandable form.

Rather than storing isolated documents, the objective is to create an interconnected enterprise knowledge fabric that supports intelligent search, Digital Twins, AI agents, decision support, automation, and future Quantum AI applications.

---

# Vision

The vision of this repository is to develop a reusable semantic knowledge framework that enables organizations to:

- Capture enterprise knowledge
