# Overview

The **MIT Topics** repository captures educational concepts, topic mappings, practical notes, and engineering interpretations inspired by publicly available academic resources and practical experience.

Its purpose is to bridge theoretical foundations with enterprise engineering, helping students, engineers, researchers, and technology professionals understand how fundamental concepts can be applied within AI, Quantum AI (QAI), Digital Twins, Knowledge Graphs, and enterprise modernization.

This repository serves as an educational knowledge asset within the FAEP ecosystem.
