# Enterprise Models

> **FAEP Knowledge Assets | Enterprise Architecture | Organizational Intelligence**

---

# Overview

The **Enterprise Models** repository captures the architectural models that describe how organizations are structured, governed, operated, modernized, and evolved within the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

Rather than focusing on software systems alone, Enterprise Models describe the relationships between business structures, governance, practices, capability ownership, platforms, products, research, modernization programs, and ecosystem collaboration.

These models provide a reusable reference architecture for designing intelligent, scalable, and federated enterprises capable of adopting Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI).

---

# Vision

Develop a reusable enterprise architecture knowledge base that enables organizations to design, govern, modernize, and continuously improve intelligent enterprises using standardized reference models.

# Why Enterprise Models?

Enterprise transformation requires more than technology.

Organizations must understand:

- Who owns what?
- How are capabilities organized?
- How do practices collaborate?
- How are products governed?
- How do business verticals interact?
- How are modernization programs managed?
- How are ecosystem partners integrated?

Enterprise Models answer these questions.
