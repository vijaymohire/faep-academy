# Theory Mapping

> **FAEP Knowledge Assets | Theory-to-Engineering | Research-to-Enterprise Transformation**

---

# Overview

The **Theory Mapping** repository captures the progression of ideas from scientific theory through engineering, product development, enterprise modernization, and industrial transformation.

Innovation rarely begins with a finished product. It evolves through multiple stages including scientific discovery, mathematical modeling, engineering design, validation, platform development, commercialization, and enterprise adoption.

This repository documents those relationships, enabling learners and organizations to understand how theoretical concepts become practical solutions within the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

---

# Vision

Develop a reusable knowledge framework that connects scientific theories with engineering practices, enterprise architectures, products, services, and modernization programs.

# Why Theory Mapping?

Scientific knowledge alone is often insufficient for enterprise adoption.

Organizations also need to understand:

- How research becomes engineering.
- How engineering becomes products.
- How products become enterprise capabilities.
- How modernization transforms organizations.

Theory Mapping provides this complete innovation pathway.
