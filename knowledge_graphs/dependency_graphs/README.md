# Dependency Graphs

> **FAEP Knowledge Assets | Dependency Analysis | Enterprise Relationship Intelligence**

---

# Overview

The **Dependency Graphs** repository captures the relationships and dependencies that exist between enterprise frameworks, technologies, products, engineering disciplines, business capabilities, governance models, and modernization initiatives.

Modern enterprises are highly interconnected. Understanding these dependencies enables organizations to perform impact analysis, architecture planning, technology roadmapping, governance, and intelligent decision support.

Rather than viewing enterprise assets in isolation, Dependency Graphs describe **how one asset influences, enables, or depends upon another**.

---

# Vision

Develop a reusable enterprise dependency model that provides complete visibility into relationships across the FAEP ecosystem.

The objective is to improve enterprise transparency, support modernization, reduce risk, and enable AI/QAI systems to reason over complex enterprise dependencies.

# Why Dependency Graphs?

Traditional documentation often explains **what** a technology is.

Dependency Graphs explain:

- What depends on it?
- What will be affected if it changes?
- What capabilities require it?
- What frameworks support it?
- What products consume it?
- What modernization activities use it?

This transforms documentation into enterprise intelligence.
