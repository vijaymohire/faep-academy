# Concept Graphs

> **FAEP Knowledge Assets | Semantic Concepts | Enterprise Knowledge Foundation**

---

# Overview

The **Concept Graphs** repository captures the fundamental concepts used throughout the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

Before organizations can build intelligent Digital Twins, Knowledge Graphs, AI Agents, or Quantum AI (QAI) systems, they must establish a shared understanding of the concepts that define their enterprise.

Concept Graphs provide this semantic foundation by identifying important concepts, defining their meaning, and describing how they relate to one another across engineering, research, governance, modernization, and enterprise operations.

---

# Vision

Create a reusable enterprise concept library that enables humans and intelligent systems to understand, connect, and reason about knowledge consistently across the FAEP ecosystem.

---

# Objectives

This repository aims to:

- Standardize enterprise terminology
- Capture reusable knowledge

- Enable semantic interoperability
- Improve AI and QAI reasoning
- Reduce ambiguity
- Accelerate enterprise modernization

---

# Why Concept Graphs?

Traditional documentation often stores knowledge as isolated documents.

Concept Graphs organize knowledge as interconnected concepts.

Instead of:

```
Digital Twin

(standalone document)
```

Concept Graphs represent relationships:

```
Digital Twin
        │
        ├── Knowledge Graph
        ├── Enterprise
        ├── Product
        ├── Lifecycle
        ├── AI Agent
        ├── Data
        └── Governance
```

This enables semantic understanding instead of simple keyword matching.
