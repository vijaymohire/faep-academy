# Overview

The **QAI Topics** repository captures the major knowledge domains that contribute to Quantum Artificial Intelligence (QAI) within the FAEP (Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform) ecosystem.

Quantum AI represents the convergence of quantum computing, artificial intelligence, high-performance computing, systems engineering, enterprise architecture, and intelligent automation.

This repository organizes these interdisciplinary topics into reusable knowledge assets that support education, research, engineering, enterprise modernization, and future commercial solutions.
