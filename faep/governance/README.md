# FAEP Governance Architecture

## Overview

The **FAEP Governance Architecture** establishes the architectural services responsible for policy management, compliance, trust, risk management and lifecycle oversight within the Federated Autonomous Ecosystem Platform (FAEP).

Governance provides the mechanisms that ensure intelligent systems remain secure, explainable, compliant and aligned with organizational objectives throughout their lifecycle.

Within the FAEP architecture, governance coordinates policies across architectural fabrics, control planes, registries, orchestration services and runtime libraries.

---

# Vision

Develop a governance architecture that enables trusted, transparent and resilient intelligent ecosystems while supporting enterprise transformation, scientific research and autonomous cyber-physical systems.

The long-term objective is to establish governance as a reusable architectural capability that spans every layer of the FAEP ecosystem.

---
