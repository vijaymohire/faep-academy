# FAEP Systems Engineering

## Overview

The **Systems Engineering** library provides the principles, methodologies and engineering practices required to design, develop, validate and evolve complex multidisciplinary systems throughout their lifecycle.

Systems Engineering integrates stakeholder needs, requirements, architecture, design, implementation, verification, validation, operations and lifecycle management into a coherent engineering discipline.

Within the FAEP Academy, Systems Engineering serves as the foundation that connects software engineering, hardware engineering, artificial intelligence, quantum computing and enterprise engineering into a unified engineering methodology.

One thing I would do differently here is make it INCOSE-centric while extending it into QAI Systems Engineering. That way, the repository starts from established systems engineering practices and then introduces your own extensions, rather than appearing disconnected from mainstream systems engineering.

# Systems Engineering Methods

The library includes dedicated guidance for:

## axiomatic_design/

A systematic design methodology that minimizes complexity while maintaining functional independence.

---

## decision_based_design/

Engineering decision methodologies supporting trade-off analysis, optimization and informed system design.

---

## incose/

Systems Engineering principles, lifecycle guidance and best practices aligned with the International Council on Systems Engineering (INCOSE).

---

## lean/

Lean engineering methods focused on maximizing value while minimizing waste throughout the engineering lifecycle.

---

## mission_engineering/

Mission-oriented systems engineering supporting capability planning, operational analysis and mission success.

---

## set_based_design/

Concurrent engineering methodology that evaluates multiple design alternatives before selecting the optimal solution.

---

## supervisory_control/

Architectures and engineering methods for supervisory control, monitoring, automation and human-machine collaboration.

---

## uaf/

Unified Architecture Framework (UAF) supporting enterprise, defense and large-scale system architecture modeling.
